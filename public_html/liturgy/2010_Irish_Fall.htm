Pagan Pride Day L.A. – September 26, 2010

A Cairn for the Worlds

By Z.L. Bennett

 

I. Preliminary Work

- Chime 3x3

 

- Purification of Space, treating with the Outdwellers and calling on a Protector.

(All present are already in a circle – they are asked to turn outward keeping their circle as Outdwellers are addressed to the South.  During this time censing and aspersing of the space will take place within the circle.)

 

-Outdwellers (Jonathan)

D1:  Dwellers beyond our boundaries, we offer this to you who would pit your will against ours and we ask that you trouble neither this rite, nor those gathered here this day.  (offering of grain is made at outdweller area, signified by a skull).  Outdwellers, accept our sacrifice. All:  Accept our sacrifice.

 

-Protector (Heather)

D1:  Golden Lugh, of the strong, long arm and straight spear, we ask that you join us here and lend your protection to our work this day. (offering of ale to the North, outside the circle to the warrior area, signified by a spear). 

Samildanach, accept our sacrifice.  All: Accept our sacrifice. 

 

PD1:  Turn now and face the center.

 

- Opening Statement and Precedence:

 

PD1:  Dear Pagan Folk, We gather in this place to share circle with each other, to honor the gods and allies, and to thank the Land for nurturing us.  Together let us work constructively to quicken global transformation and healing, to gently remove what does not serve the Great Mother and all her children.  So let us turn to the Old Ways with devotion and add another wave to the ocean of spirit for the good of all, while harming none.

 

Let us begin by preparing ourselves and this space.  We call first upon the Great Mother and the Powers of Inspiration to guide us.

 

-Earth Mother- (Kaila) (Heartbeat Drumming here)

 

    Great Mother of this Land and the Great Mothers, from which all our peoples have their source, we call to you.  Our ancestors have traveled far and wide and you have always sustained them, as you do us now, with your rich, fine body and a myriad of treasures.  Dear One, whisper close here, and with the grasses and trees, we will submit gladly to your cool, sweet breath.  We will swoon in your loving embrace. 

   O Great Queen,  who laughs in animal songs, who thrashes in quakes and storms, surveys on hawk pinions, and dances to tribal drums, we, your children remember you, and we wish to do you honor.   Earth Mother, woman of many faces, hopeful, ardent, and wise, Join us at our rite and accept our offering. (Offering of corn (3 ears) one to each of the three hallows).

 

- Powers of Inspiration – (Melissa) (Harp only here)

D1:  A Brid, Lady of Inspiration who manifests the divine to us in this world through vision, shaping, and speech.  Alight here, to guide us in your beauty.  May our words and actions be sweet and true to the gods and the spirits all around us.

    Brigid, Join us and accept our offering.  (Offer oil to the fire and the anvil placed near the fire on the spiral rock.)        ALL:  Join us, and accept our offering. 

 

(Hit anvil three times)

D1: Imbas upon us!      ALL:  Imbas upon us!

 

-Purification of the Folk

PD2: We will now prepare and purify ourselves for our work.

(Participants will chant the following while sensing and aspersing takes place.)

 

PD1 & 2:        From ancient waters we are reborn,

                        From ancient fires we create form.

With water and fire we now take part,

                        To cleanse our minds and cleanse our hearts.

 

 

Folk follows with:           Sacred water, sacred fire,

                                         Sacred well and sacred pyre.

                                         We are here of our own desire,

                                         Vanquish all that does conspire.

 

II. Opening of the Grove

PD2:  Now that we have purified ourselves, let us now look within to establish the sacred grove in this place and within ourselves.  Let us raise the mists of all time.

- Grove Attunement – Two Powers and the mists (Using Ian Corrigan’s Nine Breaths for axis attunement, horizontal attunement use of mists).

 

- Attuning with the Airts and/or Land, Sea, and Sky

PD1: May there be peace in the East,

       May there be peace in the South,

       May there be peace in the West, 

       May there be peace in the North,

       May there be peace in the Center, the axis of all Worlds, and the Threshold of Myth and

       Magic. (Standing in center wand, spear, or staff is pointed toward each direction).

 

PD1: And may there be peace in Land, Sea, and Sky, in Body, Mind, and Spirit. (With wand, spear, or staff a triskel is drawn overhead in the center of the Nemeton with the following words).

Let there be peace in the known (1st spiral of triskel drawn),

The unknown (2nd spiral of triskel drawn),

The realms that are far beyond our reach (3rd spiral of triskel drawn),

And those that are all around us (a large circle deosil drawn above).

*Inspired and taken in part from work of Ian Corrigan.

 

 

- Honoring the Hallows (Well, Fire. and Tree) (silence no music)

PD2:  Let us now sanctify the hallows from which we make offering.

 

(Three Druids come forward and go to their chosen hallow.  They will remain until all hallows are honored)

 

D1: (Ren)  Cauldron of the Underworld and the Chthonic Ones, Portal to the Ancestors, Dark churning waters of mystery, chaos, and wisdom, we sanctify you here with reverence and offer this to you. (offer silver to well)

 

D2: (Meg) Fire of the Heavens and Shining Ones, Portal to the Gods and Great Masters, Bright tongue of inspiration, order, and will, we sanctify you here with reverence and offer this to you. (offer oil to fire)

 

D3:  (Jack) Ancient World Tree, of the Mid-Realms and the Sidhe, Portal to all the worlds, Sturdy keeper of the Land, of history and time, we sanctify you here with reverence and offer this to you.   (offer smaller painted stone to tree)

 

D1: (Still standing at hallow)  Sacred Well, flow deep within us!       All: (Repeat).

D2: (Still standing at hallow)  Sacred Fire, burn bright within us!    All: (Repeat).

D3: (Still standing at hallow)  Sacred Tree, stand tall within us!  All: (Repeat).

 

-Sanctifying the Cairn at the Center (Harp only here)

 

D: (Liz) Ancient stones, cairn of the Otherworld, portal to the landscapes of life and death, - womb of the spirit, cultivator of noble wisdom, cave where the waters of the earth flow and the fertile earth embraces the dead, bearing new life.  We sanctify you here with reverence and offer this.  We set you apart as sacred for this time, the center of all sacred centers.  (offering of flowers, set around the cairn)

 

Sacred Cairn, grow strong within us!  All: (Repeat)

 

- Opening of the Gates (Jason)

  (This can be done another way by an experienced Gate Opener.)

PD2:  Now that we have prepared ourselves and this place, let us open the portals for our work.  We call upon our Gatekeeper, Mhannain Mac Lir.

 

D:         Masterful Prince, ride your gilded chariot, and come gently to us.

            Piercing the elegant blue of seductive, turquoise seas

            Deep in the dappled mist of ancient spiraled threes,

            Where the breath holds still, in the journey.

           

            Move toward your seekers here and open the mystery,

            Where all worlds flow as one, and let us now, together, open the gates.

 

             Gatekeeper, Accept now this gift of silver  and let our words sound in the depths.

             D offers silver triskel to the well uses hands or wand to direct

             energy to each hallow as…a grove sacrificer adds oil to the well)

            Let our offerings rise in the fire.      

 (a grove sacrificer adds oil to the fire)

            Let the devotion in our hearts be carried to all the worlds.

 (a grove sacrificer puts oil on bile and the cairn)

(Toning or movement, could be done here to build energy and accentuate the opening in a strong way).

 

A Mhannain Mac Lir, Osclaitear na Geatai anois.  Let the Gates be Open!

 

ALL: Let the Gates be Open! (Hold with brief pause so that the energies can be felt more

           acutely by the attendees).

                                               

III. Offering to the Powers

PD2:  We now call to the Kindred to join us.

- Honoring the Kindreds (Ancestors, Nature Spirits, Shining Ones – each spoken from the heart.  Each should do the following:  1) Evoke images for the folk; 2) honor/praise deity;  3) Ask them for their presence here; 4) Give offering  (all while keeping as brief as possible :o).

 

Ancestors: (Jonathan) ... Join us here, and accept our sacrifice. (drumming lightly)

Nature Spirits (Melissa)... Join us here and accept our sacrifice. (flute joins in)

Shining Ones (Sara) ... Join us here and accept our sacrifice. (harp joins in)

 

- Calling on the Deities of Occasion: An Callieach and the Dark Mothers

 

PD1: We call upon An Callieach, Dark Mother, who is found at the center of the mysteries and at the center of each heart.

 

D1: (Ren) Callieach Bhéara, Venerated Crone,

        We ask you come into our midst,

        As the earth stills -  still our minds -

        So that we may find peace in your garden. (PD offers hazelnuts to well)

       

        Veiled in the dark night of the soul,

        Our reflection in autumn and winter,

        Our beauty in summer and spring,

        As the wheel turns -  open our eyes -

        So that we may never fear your garden.  (PD offers hznuts to fire)          

 

        Dark Mother, you walk in grace with the Wise Women of all time,

        You appear here in the face of our elders,

        And are remembered in the touch of our grandmothers,

        As we grow older -  open our hearts -  

        So that we may bloom in your garden.  (PD offers hznuts to the tree)

 

An Cailleach Mhor, we offer to you with devotion, for we wish to honor and strengthen you, Join us here and accept our offering.  (PD offers hznuts to the cairn)  ALL:  Join us here and accept our offering.

 

Final Call and Reattunement

PD1:  Look within to see all the Kindred who we have called here.  Sense them standing with us, unified in our love.  Feel the presence of the Dark-cloaked Mother swirling here.  Now, take a moment to silently call to the Dark Mothers of your path from the altar of your devotion.  Offer to them within your inner temple as I offer to them now – (offering of special cedar oil to the cairn).

 

Honored Wise Ones, join us here and accept our offering.

Biodh se amhlaidh – Let it be so.  All: Repeat

 

I will now bring forth 8 the many of faces of An Cailleach from among the folk here to help us in our work. 

 

After offering is made – PD will choose 6 women from the crowd to join the 2 designated women from the grove to make 8.  They will represent the Wise Women of all Directions and the Wheel of the Year.  They will form the inner circle.  They will be brought forth (after asking their permission), honored (with necklaces whose pendant will be a wrapped black stone- youngest girls of grove will offer these to them), and then, they will be directed to sit on the blankets of different colors which were already placed around the cairn at the very center of the circle.

 

- Main Offering: 

 

PD2: As Pagans we well know our connection to the Earth, and we are called by our traditions to live in harmony and right relationship with Her.  Today, in the presence of An Callieach and the Great Mothers and Grandmothers we have called forth from all our hearts, we will journey beneath the veils of illusion - into the depths – so that we may find purpose and clarity for our healing work this day.  Once found, we will seek transformation of what no longer serves the world and we will replace what is removed through the offering of our devotion and our blessings.  We offer, this day, our strength to the Wise Ones whose charge it is to guide all spirits through the dark passages of the spiral into a new, illuminated time.

2) Brief Trance Journey – (Ren will drum here only)

PD1 will lead trance. 

Preliminary Work: Re-attune with those present, recall the two powers, walk the mists into the inner temple.

 

Outer Circle

(Representing the outer world) – & Warrior will walk around outer perimeter

Goal: To go within the inner temple to seek out wisdom from the dark mothers.  What global thoughtforms are blocking the world from better relationship with the earth and with all its creatures?  Choose one image, idea, etc. and bring it back as a bearer to the circle.

 

Inner Circle

(Representing the Dark Mothers, Wise women/elders of the tribes of the earth) – Goal:  To go within the inner temple/cairn to seek the blessings and wisdom from the dark mothers.  What thoughtforms and blessings should replace the void of thoughtforms and blockages to be removed?  Choose one image, idea, etc. and bring it back as a bearer to the inner circle.

 

3)After Trance Journey/Charging:

PD1: Charge the piece of yarn given with this thoughtform/blessing and wrap around twig and tie off.   When ready step forward and hand your work to the Wise Ones of the inner circle here.

  (Soft Drumming – rhythm moves from heartbeat – as soon as the offerings begin when done the drumming will move back to a heartbeat)

PD1:  Dear ladies of the inner circle, hold your blessing twigs to your heart and when ready offer them into the cairn to replace what will be removed with love.

 

5)Energy Offering

PD1: Now, we will amplify this work as a weaver’s beam, through song and dance.  Let us send the Great Mothers all the energy, power, and love we can to strengthen and nourish them and their work which is now joined with ours.  

 

Bards:(raising energy to apex through Earth Mother chant – up to improvisational pitch and strength) (After chant begins, drumming will join to build with energy and grow louder – allow for dancing too – cue to end will come from the women of the inner circle – with a definitive hands up and released)

 

Those grove members in Inner Circle will instruct the other women in the circle to remain seated while singing chant, holding hands and swaying, raising them up as a cone of energy when appropriate. 

 

PD2:  Dear Mother of us All, May our work find honor in your eyes.  Hear your children and know our love.  An Callieach and Wise Women accept our offering. 

All: Repeat

IV. The Blessing

- Calling for Blessing

PD1:  Now it is a happy time indeed, when we receive the blessings of the Kindred into each of our lives.   We begin first by asking for the waters of life (which will be water with a shot of whiskey).

 

D1: (Meg) Callieach Beare and the Dark Mothers and Wise Women of our hearts, we have offered here to you and have put your guidance into action to serve the earth and your children.  And so we ask, Great Mothers, Grant us the waters. 

 

All:  Great Mothers, Grant us the Waters.

 

D2: (Liz) Shining Ones, and Ancestors, we have remembered you here and we stand strong proclaiming our pride in the Ancient Ways at this Pagan Pride Day.  As we seek to keep the Old Ways strong and forge new ground in partnership with you, we ask, Kindred Three, grant us the waters. 

 

All:  Kindred Three Grant us the Waters.

 

D3: (Kaila) Nature Spirits, we have sought health, joy, and peace for you this day, may we always be in good relationship.  In offering you our alliance we ask, Nature Spirits Grant us the Waters.

 

All:  Nature Spirits, Grant us the Waters

 

PD2:  Do you accept the Blessings offered here in this sacred space? 

Then let us celebrate, for these are the Waters of Life! –

 

PD1: And now holding these blessings in my hands, I pour the Blessings of the gods onto these acorns to instill their blessings upon you so that each of you may carry them from this place as a testament and reminder to the work you did here this day.  Let us stand for a moment in silence as each of you receives the Kindreds gifts.   Let these little acorns serve as a touchstone for further inspiration in your work that will serve all the worlds.  These acorns are blessings to be wished upon, so ask the gods for what you need.  Harp only – acorns are passed out

PD2: Happily we have offered to the Great Mothers and the Kindred and gratefully we have received their blessings.  Let us now thank the powers that have aided us in our rite. 

 (Ren)        An Callieach, Dark Mothers

       (Mike)      Kindred Three

       (Heather) Protector

- Thanking Gatekeeper and Closing the Gates

PD1:  We now call upon our Gatekeeper once more.

 

D: A Mhannain Mac Lir, Lord of the mists through which we travel, for holding the gates open with us and keeping vigil over our steps, Go raibh maith agat, we thank you.   

Now, Ageless One, we ask that you work your will with ours so that we may close the gates while allowing this sacred time to linger here as we walk in community here. 

Let the well now be, the water that sustains us.

Let the fire now be, the flame that warms our nights.

Let the tree now be our shelter and comfort when we gather under its leafy boughs.

Let the cairn now be chards of once strong mountains.

 

A Mhannnain Mac Lir, Dúntar na Geataí anois!  Let the Gates Be Closed!    ALL: Repeat.

 

PD1:  Regrounding and Centering

PD2:  Thank you for joining us here… and as we began, so let us end.
