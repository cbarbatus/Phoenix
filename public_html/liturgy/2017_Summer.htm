INITIATING THE RITE

 

Processional Chant/Purification

 

Siobhan: Druids release the cares of your mundane life.  Take a deep breath in and allow your mind to be still.  Thoughts outside of this time are released.  You are here spiraling ever forward in the present.

 

Mike:Feel the pull of our sacred space, calling you, whispering on the wind.  In this space we create our sacred realities.  We can become all the Kindreds believe us to be.  Purification allows us to transform into our sacred selves.  Please come forward, be purified, and attune yourself to mythic time.  Let go and cross the divide.

 

No Chant, folk will silently process to outside, and Walk between twin flames for purification.

 

GUARDIAN

 

Siobhan: Druids!  Welcome to the Summer Fires - Ivanjski Krijesovi [ee-vhan-yis-kee  kree-yes-o-vee] - ritual of Raven’s Cry Grove, ADF!  We stand here at the threshold.  Warrior, call a guardian for our rite.

 

Heather: [standing in the North]

 

Mighty, Perun [pay-roon], thunderer!

Keeper of the axe and lightning bolt,

You who protects your people from all harm,

We call upon you for assistance.

To guard us from any energies or forces,

that do not uphold our rite.

Oh great striker, Perun,

Keep these baleful forces at bay,

Allow us to enter our sacred space in safety and peace.

Offering of wine

Neka bude! [nay-kah boo-deh]

 

 

Siobhan: Here we create our threshold.  We open the roadway to the sacred center.  Warrior, mark this space that the outdwellers shall not enter.

Warrior draws a line at the entrance.

Heather:  Perun [pay-roon] - we ask you to watch this threshold.

 

 

Outdwellers

 

Siobhan: Druids, as we prepare to make offering to the Outdwellers, please avert your eyes so as not to form a relationship with them, and also look to the Outdwellers in your own hearts.  This is a celebration of love.  All that we carry into the nemeton today will flower.  Take only that which you wish to root, grow, and flourish.

 

Warrior!  Make offering and appease the Outdwellers who do not support our ways.

 

Heather:

Mighty spirits of chaos,

dwellers of the outer dark,

who have stood against the gods.

You who care not for our ways,

take this offering in honor and respect

and trouble not our working here this day.

Statement Of Purpose

 

Siobhan: Today we gather between the Earth and Sky.

To worship and honor the Kindred as one people.

To sing their praise and make our sacrifice.

As we seek their blessings in return.

 

Mike: We are here to celebrate the Summer Solstice - The Longest Day, as our Ancestors did for generations before us, and as our children will do, generations hence. This evening, we celebrate the marriage of Jarilo [yaw-ree-low]  and Mara [mah-rah], twin children of mighty Perun.  Their marriage brings a bountiful harvest.

 

Siobhan: As our ancestors did and our children may do, so do we now: we are here to

honor the Old Bargain between mortals and the Kindreds.  So let us join together as one folk to make our offerings in joy and reverence.

 

Neka bude!

 

All: Neka bude! [nay-kah boo-deh]

KEY OFFERINGS

 

Invocations of the Deities of the Occasion

SVAROVICH/SVAROG

Siobhan:

Svarovich [ssvar-oh–veech], Great Smith!

Your forge's fires are burning bright,

As the sun's might grows at summer's height.

Our own passions are set afire,

As we join in love, joy and delight.

Watch over this wedding of Day and Night,

We call on you,  Shining God of Steel,

As we celebrate the year's turning wheel.

Svarovich [ssvar-oh–veech],

Prihvatite našu žrtvu [pree-h’vah-dee-tay  nah-shu  zheh-tu]

Celebrate with us in this rite!

Neka Bude!  [nay-kah boo-deh]

 

JARILO

Mike:

Jarilo [yaw-ree-low]  White God of Spring!

Horse God, Son of Mighty Perun,

You have emerged from the Underworld from Veles' grasp,

Victorious and ready to seek your bride.

The earth has greened and the pulse of life has quickened,

Now that winter's grip is but a memory.

Summer's Fires are now at their height, and you at your prime.

Join now in union with your bride of Winter, Mara,

So that the cycle may continue and the balance be maintained,

For now is the time of light and flame,

The time of youth, energy and passion

Now is the time to celebrate the goodness of life!

Prihvatite našu žrtvu, [pree-h’vah-dee-tay  nah-shu  zheh-tu] cheerful Jarilo [yaw-ree-low] ,

Celebrate with us in this rite!

Neka Bude!  [nay-kah boo-deh]

 

MARA

Siobhan:

Mara [mah-rah],  Goddess of the Eternal Cycle,

Now the nights grow shorter and the days warmer,

For your cold heart has warmed at the return of your lover.

See him now as he rides over the fields,

Jarilo, the youthful god of springtide.

Join him on this most auspicious wedding day.

May our sacrificial fires be pleasing to you,

May they purify us and make us whole.

May your union be long and fruitful,

So that the harvest be bountiful and the earth renewed.

Prihvatite našu žrtvu, [pree-h’vah-dee-tay  nah-shu  zheh-tu] dark Mara,

Celebrate with us in this rite!

Neka Bude! [nay-kah boo-deh]

RECEIVING THE BLESSING

 

Siobhan:

Druids, all our offerings, all we have done so far in our rite, have led to this moment.  This is magic time!  We have made offerings to the Powers, and now we shall ask for their blessings in return.  Visualize the blessings that each of you desire and need from the Kindreds.  See it clearly in your mind’s eye. And when we ask for the Waters of Life, when we say, “Give us the Waters of Life”, see the blessing you desire descend into the upraised cup like a mist, filling the cup.  And when we drink of the Waters, you will receive your blessing.  So close your eyes and take a moment to visualize your needs, and the needs of our Grove, that we may all receive the blessings we desire.

 

Allow time for the people to meditate on needs and desires.  Lead2 pours Croatian Wine into the cup and holds it.

 

Siobhan:O Ancient, Noble and Shining Ones!  We have offered to you, so we ask for your gifts in return.

Mike: elevates the Waters.

Siobhan:Hallow these waters!  Bless our lives with Magic, Power and Abundance as we drink of your essence.  Kindreds, we ask you, give us the waters of life!

 

All: Give us the waters of life!

 

A pause…

 

Siobhan: Behold the waters of life!  Druids, drink of the waters of life!

 

Song: Pour the Waters one time, then drumming and music etc.
