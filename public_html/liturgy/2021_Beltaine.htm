INITIATING THE RITE

 

The folk assemble outside the nemeton to observe the preparations

 

 

Guardian

 

Mike: Druids!  Welcome to the Beltaine /BELL-tin-uh/ ritual of Raven’s Cry Grove, ADF!  We stand here at the threshold.  Warrior, call a guardian for our rite.

 

Warrior: [standing in the North]

 

Dagda /DAHG-duh/ father of the gods,

Lord of the Coire, the cauldron of plenty, from which none hath left unsatisfied.

Wielder of the club that brings death and resurrection.

Wield your club for us this day, keep vigil over the ones who would seek to disrupt us.

 

Shining One, we would offer ale to you so you may not thirst (Offer Ale), and bread so may you may not hunger (Offer Bread).

 

Offering of Ale and Bread

 

Bíodh sé Amhlaidh! /BEE-uh SHAY OW-lee/

 

 

 

Mike: Here we create our threshold.  We open the roadway to the sacred center. 

 

Warrior, mark this space that the outdwellers shall not enter.

 

Warrior draws a line at the entrance.

 

Warrior: Dagda /DAHG-duh/- we ask you to watch this threshold.

 

 

 

Procession and Purification

 

Lisa: Druids release the cares of your mundane life.  Take a deep breath in and allow your mind to be still.  Thoughts outside of this time are released.  You are here spiraling ever forward in the present.

 

Mike: Feel the pull of our sacred space, calling you, whispering on the wind.  In this space we create our sacred realities.  We can become all the Kindreds believe us to be.  Purification allows us to transform into our sacred selves.  Please come forward, be purified, and attune yourself to mythic time.  Let go and cross the divide.

 

Form the procession with Flowers in hand.

 

Lisa:  By the light of the fire and the might of the water, make this Grove be whole and holy.

 

[Cense and Asperse During Procession]

 

Procession – Song: Come We Now as a People (By Ian Corrigan)

 

Come we now as a people,

To gather at the sacred well.

Come we now as a people

Together in the warmth and the light of the flame.

 

Folk take their place in the circle after placing Flowers on the altar. 

 

When all folk are in place,

 

 

Welcome

 

Mike: ring chime 3x3 times saying:

Ancestors! (chime 3x)

Nature Spirits! (chime 3x)

Shining Ones! (chime 3x)

 

We have crossed the threshold into sacred space.  Please reconnect with the sacred cosmos as we prepare to open the gates to the otherworlds, the place where the kindreds will reveal themselves once more.

 

 

HONORING THE EARTH MOTHER

 

Earth Mother:

Earth Mother kneeling at the well
Ancient One of ever changing beauty
Primal Mother of the Mighty Tuatha /TOO ah hah/
And Great Goddess of Sovereignty
We are renewed in your waters
Cradled within your mantle of green
And sustained by your abundance
Danu /DAN-oo/, Earth Mother, we call upon the life-giving magic of the Earth!

Great Earth Mother,
We come before you in love and respect
and we ask that you uphold and bless our gathering!

An offering of bread (1 whole round loaf) and clarified butter is made to the Earth Mother)

 

Earth Mother, be welcome among us!

 

 

 

Statement of Purpose

 

Mike: Today we gather between the Earth and Sky.

To worship and honor the Kindred as one people.

To sing their praise and make our sacrifice.

As we seek their blessings in return.

 

Lisa:  Beltaine is the fertility festival par excellence! This is indeed an

extravagant holiday. At this time of year, roses are in full bloom, peaches and berries are ripe. This is the Love Dance of the Gods, the Wedding of Heaven and Earth, the Bridal feast of the Goddess! At Beltaine, our energies are turned toward the Love and Union that brings a Harvest of new life among us.

 

Mike: As our ancestors did and our children may do, so do we now: we are here to

honor the Old Bargain between mortals and the Kindreds.  So let us join together as one folk to make our offerings in joy and reverence.

 

Bíodh sé amhlaidh! /BEE-uh SHAY OW-lee/

 

 

Inspiration

 

Rhythmic drumming to accompany the inspiration invocation

 

Lisa: Brighid /A Vreej (more Irish) or BRIDGE-id (more Americanized)/!  Great goddess.  We ask you for inspiration for our rite!

 

Inspiration:

Bright Brighid,

Daughter of the Dagda /DAHG-duh/, Flame of Inspiration,

Maker of metal, Maker of good health, Maker of song,

With you as our mother we need fear no sickness,

With you as our mother we need fear no dull swords,

With you as our mother we need fear no empty words.

Our tribe is triple-blessed, O shining light of the hearth,

and we your children ask you to alight in our hearts,

Alight in our heads, and alight in our voices,

That we may give praise to the gods with good skill.

Offer oil to the anvil placed on a rock (which has three spirals painted on it) near the fire and then hit 3x.

Brighid, Accept our offering.  Bíodh sé amhlaidh! /BEE-uh SHAY OW-lee/

 

 

 

Establish the Group Mind

 

Two Powers: [Two powers meditation]

 

 

Recreate the Cosmos

 

Mike: Druids, let us now re-establish the Sacred Center of the Worlds.

 

 

The Three Realms

 

The waters support and surround us.

The land extends about us.

The sky stretches above us.

And in the center burns a living flame.

Let us pray with a good fire.

May all the Kindred bless us

And our worship be true.

May our actions be just

And our love be pure.

Blessings and honor and worship to the holy ones.

 

 

Well, Fire, and Tree

 

Portal Song (by Ian Corrigan)

 

Chorus:

By Fire and by Water, between the Earth and Sky

We stand like the World Tree rooted deep, crowned high.

 

Well: Come we now to the Well, the eye and the mouth of Earth,

Come we now to the Well, and silver we bring.

Come we now to the Well, the waters of rebirth,

Come we now to the Well, together we sing.

An offering of silver is made to the well

 

(Repeat Chorus)

 

Fire: We will kindle a Fire, Bless all with harm to none,

We will kindle a Fire, and offering pour,

We will kindle a Fire, A light ‘neath the moon and sun,

We will kindle a Fire, our spirits will soar.

An offering of oil is made to the Fire.

 

(Repeat Chorus)

 

Tree: Gather we at the tree, the root and the crown of all,

Gather we at the Tree, below and above,

Gather we at the Tree, together we make our call,

Gather we at the Tree, in wisdom and love.

(Repeat Chorus)

 

 

Lisa:

Fire, well, and sacred tree,

Flame and flow and grow in me!

Land, sea and sky,

Bíodh sé amhlaidh! /BEE-uh SHAY OW-lee/

 

 

 

 

OPEN THE GATES

 

Gates:

Shapeshifter, Mist Lord, Sidhe /SHE/ King!

A Mhanannain Mic Lir /uh WAH-nuh-NAWN mahk LIHR/! 

We have come to meet you at the crossroads,

To walk the Sacred Way.

We ask that you share Your mysteries with us,

And ward our steps in safety, Lord of the Gates.

 

offering of oil at each of the hallows as each are addressed

 

Now, O Manannan /MAH-nuh-NAWN/, Ferryman of the Shrouded Path,

join your will with ours.

 

Let the fire be opened now, as a gate to the Shining Ones.

 

Let the well be opened now, as a gate to the Otherworld,

 

Let the tree stand as an connector of the ways,

to serve as a path to all the Worlds.

 

Open as a road to our voices so that they may be heard by the kindreds.

 

A Mhanannain Mic Lir /uh WAH-nuh-NAWN mahk LIHR/,

Osclaítear na geataí anois! /US-kleh-terr nah GATT-ee uh-NISH!/

 

Let the Gates be Open!

 

 

Lisa: Druids, we are now at the center of the cosmos.  We are woven into the sacred fabric of the universe.  Here, the Kindreds can hear our thoughts and know our hearts.  Let there only be truth here.

 

 

 

INVITING THE THREE KINDREDS

 

 

Mike:  Druids! Make our offerings to the Kindreds – the Ancient Ones, the Noble Ones, and the Shining Ones.

 

Ancestors

 

Now, we will make our offering to the Ancestors.

 

Ancestors:

Ancestors, A shinseara /uh HIN-sher-uh/

You who lie deep within this land, you whose memories hold it, and whose hearts are tied to its very essence; tribes of old, we offer you welcome!

Grandmothers and grandfathers, our beloved dead, kin of blood and heart, ancient tribes whose legacy is in our veins, we offer you welcome!

Elders and wise ones, you who guide the people, wise men and women of ancient days, we offer you welcome!

Mighty Ancestors, A shinseara /uh HIN-sher-uh/we would give you honour and praise, and we would ask that you join us here today!

 

offering of Whisky

 

Ancestors, accept our sacrifice!  Bíodh sé amhlaidh! /BEE-uh SHAY OW-lee/

 

 

Mike: Druids, close your eyes, take a moment, and feel the Ancestors as they join us here.

 

Allow a few moments

 

 

Nature Spirits

 

Now, we will make our offering to the Nature Spirits.

 

Nature Spirits:

Nature Spirits, A sprideanna na talaimh /uh SPRIJ-uh-nuh na TAHL-iv/

Spirits of the land, hear your friends and companions!

Our kindred of stone and stream, rock and fertile soil, kins of the earth, we offer you welcome!

 

Our kindred of green, herb and flower, the smallest shrubs and the mightiest trees, roots and stems and fruits; Green kin, we offer you welcome!

Our kindred of fur and feather and fin, our kindred who walk or fly or swim or crawl, A sprideanna na talaimh /uh SPRIJ-uh-nuh na TAHL-iv/

we offer you welcome!-eev

 

offering of flowers

 

Nature Spirits, accept our sacrifice!  Bíodh sé amhlaidh! /BEE-uh SHAY OW-lee/

 

Mike: Druids, close your eyes, take a moment, and feel the Nature Spirits as they join us here.

 

Allow a few moments

 

 

 

Shining Ones

 

Now, we will make our offering to the Shining Ones.

 

Shining Ones:

Shining ones, A Dheithe /uh YAY-huh/, your children call out to you, hear us eldest and brightest!

The first children of the mother, our wisest and mightiest, loving and comforting, gods and goddesses, we offer you welcome!

Gods and goddesses of this place, ancient and powerful, known or unknown to us, gods of this place, we offer you welcome!

All of the deities of those here gathered, with whom we worship, you who bless our lives, our patrons and matrons, we offer you welcome!

A Dheithe /uh YAY-huh/, we would give you honour and praise, and ask that you would join us here this day.

offering of oil

 Gods and Goddesses, accept our sacrifice!  Bíodh sé amhlaidh! /BEE-uh SHAY OW-lee/

 

 

Mike: Druids, close your eyes, take a moment, and feel the Gods and Goddesses as they join us here.

 

Allow a few moments

 

 

Praise Offerings

 

Lisa: Druids, as the spirit moves you, make praise offerings to the Kindreds or your Patrons.  Join us in song and do whatever would please the Kindreds.

 

 

KEY OFFERINGS

 

 

 

Invocation of the Deities of the Occasion

 

 

Invocation of Aengus Og

 

Aengus Og [ENG-guss OHG], God of youthful love and beauty.  Bard, who brings joy with the lyrical tones of music.  You who were captured by love and gave all that you were to see that love fulfilled. You, who followed your heart and the sound of your lover’s wings into the places between the worlds.  You allowed love to guide you to your own sovereignty.  You followed her haunting laughter and were inspired to follow your dreams. Grant us your presence Aengus Og [ENG-guss OHG].  Allow us to follow you with love; to dance to your tune as we enter the liminal spaces.

 

 

 

Invocation of Cáer

 

Caer Ibormeith  [KIRE ih-VOR-muh], the Celtic Goddess of prophecy, dreams, and liminal spaces.  You, who spends one year in human form and then transforms on Samhain, experiencing the death of your humanity.  The next year of your life is spent in the form of the swan, along with your 150 maiden friends who take the path of death and rebirth along with you, their guide.  One of the original shamans, you embrace your animal nature and take flight with your horde.  We welcome your flight, Caer Ibormeith [KIRE ih-VOR-muh].  Join us now in our rite!

OMEN

 

Mike: Seer, what blessings and wisdom do the Kindreds offer us in return?

 

Seer: takes the omen

 

Mike:

Druids, all our offerings, all we have done so far in our rite, have led to this moment.  This is magic time!  We have made offerings to the Powers, and now we shall ask for their blessings in return.  Visualize the blessings that each of you desire and need from the Kindreds.  See it clearly in your mind’s eye. And when we ask for the Waters of Life, when we say, “Give us the Waters of Life”, see the blessing you desire descend into the upraised cup like a mist, filling the cup.  And when we drink of the Waters, you will receive your blessing.  So close your eyes and take a moment to visualize your needs, and the needs of our Grove, that we may all receive the blessings we desire.

 

Allow time for the people to meditate on needs and desires.  Mike pours May Wine into the cup and holds it.

 

Mike: O Ancient, Noble and Shining Ones!  We have offered to you, so we ask for your gifts in return.

 

Mike elevates the Waters.

 

Hallow these waters!  Bless our lives with Magic, Power and Abundance as we drink of your essence.  Kindreds, we ask you, give us the waters of life!

 

 

A pause…

 

Mike: Behold the waters of life!  Druids, drink of the waters of life!

 

Song: Pour the Waters one time, then drumming and music etc.

 

 

AFFIRMATION OF THE BLESSING

 

Mike: We have drunk of the Waters of Life!  The Powers have given us true and wondrous blessings.  We affirm the gifts of our Kindreds and acknowledge their power in our lives.

 

Druids, do you accept the gifts of the Gods?
