Pre-ritual instruction notes:

 

In honor of the waves we’re going to do something a little different today -- we’re going to hum. We’re going to breathe audibly and hum together -- through the entire ritual. Let’s try it out -- a gentle hum, not trying to hold it as long as possible, just a gentle vocalization as you breathe. And together it makes a constant sound. Make it as gentle as possible - we’re not singing, just making some waves. Now try this -- as I’m speaking... your hum becomes so gentle, barely audible... and when I stop... you give your hum a bit more volume... you fill my silence.

 

There will be a bit less talking today - the rite will be a bit shorter - I’m not trying to give anyone a sore throat, or make you feel winded... if you need to stop and breathe without sound please do. But if we work together we can keep the waves going, keep the power flowing, and by the end of the rite we’ll all have a fabulous oxygen high!

 

We have two songs to learn. Very short songs. In ritual I’ll stop speaking... we’ll all be breathing and humming... then when I start this chant you all join in however you’re feeling it. We’ll play with it a while and let it fade on it’s own back into our soft hum.

 

The first song is “The Ocean is the Beginning of the Earth” **teach chant**.

 

The second song is the background harmony for Manannan’s Song, which is a piece I wrote a few years ago and if you’ve seen my open the gates you’ll recognize the tune. **teach chant and variations**

 

We’re also going to have a few word chants. They are written in your program but you shouldn’t need to look at it. Someone will say a set of four lines, and you start repeating them back, but it’s not meant to be unison, it’s meant to be ripples on the sea. Try saying it slower than the person next to you, then faster. Try repeating just one of the lines, one of the words. Play with it, move with it... then let it fade back to hum.

 

Finally there’s going to be a guided meditation after The Manannan Song. As the song finishes I’m going to give a dramatic gesture like this for everyone to lay down and get comfortable. I recommend laying with your head towards the center so you can hear me better. Once you are lying down and settled in turn your attention to your breath and you can stop vocalizing.

 

After the meditation a bowl of ocean water will come around and you’ll breathe your offering onto it. See the ripples on the water as you exhale -- it is Manannan’s ear -- and know that you have been heard.
