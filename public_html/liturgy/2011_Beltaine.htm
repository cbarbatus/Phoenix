- Opening Statement and Precedence:

PD:  Brothers and sisters, welcome to this celebration of the Full Moon – The Wind Moon – where we will move the winds to fan the flames of divine ecstasy, shaping that energy to bolster our work the whole year through.  

 

As we belong to the gods and the gods belong to us, let us begin by praying with a good fire.  Let us offer with a full heart, knowing that when we do, we summon the gods’ favor and nurture our relationship with them.  In remembrance, we strengthen their powers.  In honor, we balance the worlds.  In love, we bring peace and keep the Old Ways alive and present. 

 

We call first upon the Great Mother and the Powers of Inspiration.

- Statement of Purpose -

 

 PD:  We grow in anticipation as the fires of Beltane grow stronger within us, moving us to unleash our passions and to burn bright-hot.  But, the fires of Beltane that will join the fires of our bodies are meant for something more than mere revelry, indulgence, or madness, as lovely as they can be for a time.  These fires offer us fuel for the year ahead.  They burn away the of old while breathing new life, purpose, and illumination, into the crucible of our bodies. 

 

     Tonight we will prepare, warm, and turn, the cauldron-centers within us.  In the Irish way, we will learn as divine poets learn, by cultivating the divine fires of inspiration in the loins, the heart, and the head and by joining in the divine act of creation and reciprocity.  Let us shape the mettle of our work and our journey by exploring how to temper the wondrous gift of the Beltane fire, tending it for the long run, and measuring it wisely out to fit -  like a bard bends ecstatic waves into the ebb and flow of meter, music, and magic.   

 

     Let us then, stand in the Celtic idea of the “fitness of things” where beauty and truth illuminate all endeavors and the dynamic of the spoken word orders the universe.

            Where the breath holds still, in the journey.

            Move toward your seekers here and open the mystery,

-Main Offering-

      

PD:  Here is the altar of the sacred center, where the crossroads meet in this Nemeton, in us, and in all the worlds.  Tonight, we will offer to all the worlds an inner spark of each of the fires that burns within us.  

(Reveal the Three Cauldrons in the center, covered by a cloth to show- three small cauldrons placed vertically on a small shelving unit with 3 tiers.  Each tier has a cloth  a different color to represent the focus of the cauldron and to symbolize the tripartition of IE culture, Irish regions and those aspects of those things within us – Highest Cauldron –  (white), Middle Cauldron – (red), and Cauldron closer to the earth – (blue-green). One small candle is placed next to each cauldron, matching color of cloth –candle to be added into the cauldron if able.) (Slow drumming is done during the offering).

 

PD:     In the presence of the Cauldron-Keepers, the Kindred, and those who the folk has gathered here, we sanctify and open the Altar of the Three Cauldrons, the Sacred Tree in us and in all the worlds.  As each flame is lit, I call upon you, in your magical way, to freely offer up some of the warmth of the inner fire within you to the flames that will be established here.  As we warm them, we warm potential for all the worlds, and they will in turn warm us.

 

D1: Upright Cauldron of Birthing and Dying,

       As a people, we offer warmth to you from the fires in our loins,

       The center of our health, energy, and survival.  

       Let this flame warm and strengthen you in all worlds.  Accept our sacrifice.

       (Grove Sacrificer (GS) offers silver to cauldron and lights blue candle).

 

D2: Upright Cauldron of Purpose and Inspired Work,

       The center where dreams and skill meet form.

       Let this flame warm and strengthen you in all worlds.  Accept our sacrifice.  

       (GS offers a healing herb to cauldron & lights red candle)

 

D3: Upright Cauldron of Vision and Wisdom,

       As a people, we offer warmth to you, from the fires in our heads,

       The center of our creative power and illumination.  

       Let this flame warm and strengthen you in all worlds.  Accept our sacrifice.

       (GS offers white or purple stone to cauldron and lights white candle).   

 

PD: Triple Realms of all the Worlds we have offered a part of ourselves to you here.  Let our sacrifice be measured in truth and blessed in beauty.  When you are aligned and charged, the worlds are renewed and refreshed.

(offering back what is left of the blessing cup to bowl of earth which will later be returned to its original location.)

 

- Thanking and Petitioning the Gatekeeper

 

PD:  We call upon our Gatekeeper once more. 

 

D: A Mhannain Mac Lir, Lord of the mists through which we travel,
for holding the gates open with us and keeping vigil over our steps,

Now, Ageless One, by your power and our magic we end what we began.
Let the well now be, the water that sustains us.
Let the fire now be, the flame that warms us.
