INITIATING THE RITE

Procession and Purification

Angela will call the folk to the ritual by ringing the chimes.

Angela will say these words as they assemble in procession:

ANGELA: Children of the Earth! Tonight we come together to celebrate the autumnal equinox, the turning of the wheel from the bright sun, energy, vibrancy, fullness, and life, to the cold moon, sleep, contemplation, emptiness, and death. For just as all who have come before us have known, and we know now, nature’s balance is only achieved through light and dark, ecstasy and pain, life and death.

AERIES: Tonight we celebrate an adaptation of the Athenian festival of Thesmophoria, a solemn tribute to the great mother goddess Demeter, and her beloved and only daughter, Kore. Tonight we honor the depth, love, and pain of the mother-child bond, and seek to heal the Mother Wound in each of us through Kore’s descent into the Underworld, and Demeter’s agony of loss.

ANGELA: Release your hold on the mundane world. Take a deep breath in. Allow your mind to be still and your heart to be quiet. Feel the pull of our sacred space, calling you forward and inward.

AERIES: Please come forward and allow yourselves to be purified with water made sacred by fire. Let us go and cross the divide.


Folk will line up for procession. Aspersers will be at the entrance prepared to sprinkle chernips onto heads with a twig from an olive tree. Benzoin resin will burn in the incenser.  A few of the folk should carry olive branches in during procession.

Welcome

Angela will ring the chimes three times each:

ANGELA: Ancestors! (ring thrice) Nature spirits! (ring thrice) Shining ones! (ring thrice)

Children of the Earth, reconnect with the sacred cosmos as we prepare to open the gates to the otherworlds, the place where the kindreds will reveal themselves once more.


Honor the Hearth Goddess Hestia

AERIES: As we begin, we will first honor the hearth goddess, Hestia.

HESTIA: Hestia, you who tend the holy house of the lord Apollo, with soft oil dripping ever from your locks, come now into this sacred space. Come, draw near, and bestow grace upon our song.

Make an offering of wine.

HESTIA: Hestia, first and last! Éstō! [ESS-toe]

ALL: Éstō! [ESS-toe]


Guardian

ANGELA: Warrior, call a guardian for our rite!

WARRIOR: Hear us, Athena, great warrior goddess, ruler of cities and divine counselor! Sprung from the head of your father Zeus, ruler of the Olympians, clad in flashing gold armor and shaking your terrible spear, you gaze fiercely upon the battlefield with gleaming eyes and a piercing mind, aiming arrows of strategy into the heads of your chosen. Protector of the citadel and patroness of soldiers, you lend cunning and intelligence to those who fight for a just and honorable cause.

Hail to you, daughter of the wise and clever Metis! Athena, accept our offering.

ALL: Athena, accept our offering.

Make an offering of wine.

WARRIOR: Hear us, Athena. Stand guard over our rite and cast a shield of protection around our work this night.

Pour a line of sea water on the east-facing border.
Outdwellers Offering

AERIES: Let us now make an offering to the Outdwellers.

OUTDWELLERS: Outdwellers, those who oppose our work here tonight, mighty catalysts of change. We honor your place in the great balance of life, as order without chaos defies balance. We honor you that tonight’s rite may be a working of devotional magic and not destruction. In the name of balance, we offer you ambrosia, nectar of the gods, and invoke hospitality, a gift for a gift. Accept this nectar that we may do our work here in peace. Outdwellers, accept our offering.

Make an offering of ambrosia.

ALL: Outdwellers, accept our offering.


HONORING THE EARTH MOTHER

ANGELA: Hear us, O Rhea [REE-uh], Titaness daughter of Gaia and mother of the gods! We now show you honor.

Folk kneel or otherwise connect with the Earth.

EARTH MOTHER: Divine Rhea [REE-uh], mother of Hestia, Demeter, Hera, Poseidon, Hades, and Zeus, blessed queen of fertility and sacred Earth goddess, you keep under your care all that lives and grows on this Earth, with reverence and fierce protection. Mistress of the lions, bringer of sanctuary, guardian of childbirth and infancy, upon you depend the winds, the ocean, the whole Earth beneath the snowy seat of Olympus; whenever you leave the mountains and climb the great vault of heaven, Zeus himself, son of Cronus [KROW-nuhs], makes way, and all other gods likewise make way for you, Rhea [REE-uh], great and feared Earth goddess.

BARDS: “We All Come from the Goddess”

Make an offering of milk.

EARTH MOTHER: Mother Rhea [REE-uh], accept our offering.

ALL: Mother Rhea, accept our offering.

EARTH MOTHER: Éstō! [ESS-toe]

ALL: Éstō! [ESS-toe]



Statement of Purpose

AERIES: Tonight we gather between the Earth and sky to honor the Kindred as one people. We gather to sing their praise and make our offerings, as we seek their blessings in return.

ANGELA: We gather to celebrate the Autumnal Equinox, the turning from light to dark, inspired by Thesmophoria, the Athenian festival of mother and child. Tonight we honor the divine mother goddess, Demeter, her only and beloved daughter, Kore, and the bond of love and pain they shared.

AERIES: As our ancestors once did, so do we now: We are here to honor the Old Bargain between mortals and the Kindreds. Let us join together as one folk to make our offerings in reverence.

Éstō! [ESS-toe]

ALL: Éstō! [ESS-toe]


Inspiration

ANGELA: We now ask for inspiration for our rite!

BARDS: Instrumental, no percussion

INSPIRATION: Hear us, Iambe [ee-AHM-bay], you of kindly temperament, daughter of Pan and Echo, granddaughter of the noble Hermes! Iambe [ee-AHM-bay], bringer of cheer and smiles, celebrated in Attica since times of old for your bravery in saving the mournful Demeter as she wasted away in sorrow. Iambe [ee-AHM-bay], you of gracious heart, you of knowing and caring intuition, you displayed your courage in tickling the heart of the Olympian goddess by bringing light to her darkness, cheer to her pain. Come to us now, brave and clever Iambe [ee-AHM-bay], and instill in us a lightness as we travel into the dark this night.

Make an offering of milk and honey.

Iambe [ee-AHM-bay], accept our offering!

ALL: Iambe, accept our offering!

INSPIRATION: Éstō! [ESS-toe]

ALL: Éstō! [ESS-toe]

Recreate the Cosmos

The Three Realms

AERIES: Let us establish the Sacred Center.
BARDS: beat the drums rhythmically.

ANGELA: Here rests the stone called the omphalos [AHM-fuh-lohs], the navel of the world, the sacred center of all the Cosmos. Like the sacred stone at Delphi, let this stone be the place where Zeus’s eagles did meet to mark the center of the world. Let this stone mark the grave of the Pythian serpent, slain by Delian Apollo. Through this sacred center, let the World Tree grow, plunging deep within the Earth to touch the Sacred Waters below and reaching through the sky to embrace the Sacred Fires above.

Well, Fire, and Tree

WELL: We thank you, divine mother goddess Tethys [TEH-this], for this gift of the Sacred Well. From these depths flow the waters of wisdom, the same depths from which you nurtured many children.

Sacred Well, flow within us.

Make offering of silver.

ALL: Sacred Well, flow within us.

FIRE: We thank you, Hestia, for keeping the fire of the heart and hearth, the fire that burns within all of us. Bright burn the flames of the home, the flames that cleanse, the flames that promise warmth and light.

Sacred Fire, burn within us.

Make offering of rosemary.

ALL: Sacred Fire, burn within us.

OMPHALOS: Sacred Center, Holy Omphalos [AHM-fuh-lohs], Portal of the Worlds. As in antiquity, you mark the heart and core of our gathering, just as Zeus’s eagles carried you to Delphi and proclaimed you the center of our world. As you were forged with fire and emerged from the earth’s core, we feel the warmth of the sky and stretch our roots down into the Earth.

Sacred Center, let the worlds meet within us.

Make offering of fresh flowers.

ALL: Sacred Center, let the worlds meet within us.


ANGELA: The fire, the well, the sacred tree, flame and flow and grow in me!

AERIES: In land, in sea, and in sky, both below and on high! Thus is the sacred grove blessed by the gods.


OPEN THE GATES

ANGELA: Now let us open the gates.

GATEKEEPER: I call Hecate of the crossroads, guardian of the three paths, and ruler of the Middle Sky. Hecate, you hold the keys to the Cosmos. Nurturer of children, you who haunt the mountains, pray, Maiden, attend our hallowed ritual, be forever gracious to your mystic herdsmen and rejoice in our gift of garlic.

Hecate, open the gates!

Make offering of garlic to Hecate at each of the hallows as each are addressed for opening.

Let the fire be now opened, as a gate to the Shining Ones.

Let the well be now opened, as a gate to the Otherworld.

Let the omphalos [AHM-fuh-lohs] stand as a connector of the ways, through which the sacred tree grows, serving as a path to all the Worlds.

Let the gates be open!

ALL: Let the gates be open!

ANGELA: Children of the Earth, we are now at the center of the Cosmos. We are woven into the sacred fabric of the universe. Here, the Kindreds can hear our thoughts and know our hearts. Let there be only truth here.


INVITING THE THREE KINDREDS

AERIES: Druids! We now make our offerings to the Kindreds: the Ancient Ones, the Noble Ones, and the Shining Ones.

Ancestors

ANGELA: Remember the wisdom of our Ancestors as we call them.

ANCESTORS: Ancestors, you who lie deep within this land, you whose memories hold it, and whose hearts are tied to its very essence; tribes of old, we offer you welcome! Grandmothers and grandfathers, our beloved dead, kin of blood and heart, ancient tribes whose legacy is in our veins, we offer you welcome! Elders and wise ones, you who guide the people, wise men and women of ancient days, we offer you welcome! Mighty Ancestors, we give you honor and praise, and ask that you join us here tonight! We offer you welcome!

Make an offering of olive oil.

Ancestors, accept our offering.

ALL: Ancestors, accept our offering.

AERIES: Children of the Earth, close your eyes, take a moment, and feel the Ancestors as they join us here.

Pause for a few moments.

Nature Spirits

ANGELA: Now, we will draw the vitality of nature as we call the Nature Spirits.

NATURE SPIRITS: Nature Spirits, dryads of the trees and naiads of the wells, brooks, and streams, we offer you welcome! Wild ones of the woods and hills, those who swim, slither and slink, and the flying ones who soar above us all, we offer you welcome! You who grow green and fruitful, with sheltering shade and boughs, who nourish us and teach us, we offer you welcome! Nature Spirits, we give you honor and praise, and ask that you join us here tonight! We offer you welcome!

Make an offering of lemons.

Nature Spirits, accept our offering.

ALL: Nature Spirits, accept our offering.

AERIES: Children of the Earth, close your eyes, take a moment, and feel the Nature Spirits as they join us here.

Pause for a few moments.

Shining Ones

ANGELA: Feel the protections of the Shining Ones as we call them.

SHINING ONES: Shining Ones, you who we call Gods, Goddesses, Deities, Spirits, the highest and mightiest, first among the Kindreds, worthiest of hour honor, we offer you welcome! Deities of this place, known or unknown to us, we offer you welcome! Gods and Goddesses of all those here, come to our fire, we offer you welcome! Shining Ones, we give you honor and praise, and ask that you join us here tonight. We offer you welcome!

Make an offering of rosemary oil.

Shining Ones, accept our offering.

ALL: Shining Ones, accept our offering.

AERIES: Children of the Earth, close your eyes, take a moment, and feel the Shining Ones as they join us here.

Pause for a few moments.


Praise Offerings

ANGELA: Children of the Earth, reach deep within your soul, and go in the direction you are called. Come forward and make praise offerings to the Kindreds or your patrons. You may offer at the well, the fire, or the tree, sing songs, recite poetry, dance, or do whatever would please the Kindreds.

Folk make offerings as they are called.

BARDS: Instrumental


KEY OFFERINGS

AERIES: Thesmophoria was a solemn celebration for Athenian women that honored the divine mother and daughter goddesses, Demeter and Kore, and incorporated a dramatic retelling of their story. While we lack the time and space for a theatrical performance, we can honor the divine goddesses with our own brief retelling.

ANGELA: Kore was out gathering flowers when Hades, King of the Underworld and Brother to Zeus, who had lusted after Kore for a long time, used a bright yellow narcissus flower to lure her toward him. When she came close, he took her, screaming, and descended into the Underworld.

AERIES: For months, Demeter searched for her precious daughter in vain, pleading with the Olympians for help. None assisted. Only her dearest friend Hecate, daughter of Asteria, told Demeter where Kore had gone. When Demeter learned of Hades crime, she pleaded with her brother Zeus to intervene. He refused.

ANGELA: Demeter was broken. She transformed herself into an old woman and abandoned her divine duties, taking a humble role as a nursemaid to a prince. In her grief, the Earth suffered drought, famine, and death of livestock and crops.

AERIES: Upon seeing the devastation of the Earth, the Olympians intervened. It was agreed that Kore would be allowed to spend half of the year with her mother, as long as she devoted the other half to Hades, for she had eaten a few seeds of the forbidden pomegranate, an act that bound one to the Underworld forever.

ANGELA: Let us invoke and honor the Divine Mother Goddess, Demeter.

KEY: Beautiful, golden Demeter! Devoted Mother of Kore, whose glory is in the harvest, you nourish us with your bounty and abundance. We invite you to join our fire as we remember and honor the bond of love and pain between you and your precious child. We offer back to you a portion of the generous blessings we have already received, lovingly arranged to please you in the manner of a cornucopia, the very symbol of you and your gifts.

Make an offering of a cornucopia filled with grain.

Demeter, mother and provider, accept our offering!

ALL: Demeter, accept our offering!

ANGELA: Let us invoke and honor the divine goddess, Kore.

KEY: Lovely and blessed Kore! Vernal queen, whom grassy plains delight, sweet to the smell, and pleasing to the sight, you gift us with abounding fruits and the sweetest spring. We invite you to join our fire as we remember and honor the inseverable bond of child to mother. We offer you a symbol of spring’s delights—blooms for the flowers you sought—and a pomegranate to honor your sacrifice as the blessed Queen of the Underworld.

Make an offering of flowers and pomegranates.

Kore, blessed daughter, accept our offering!

ALL: Kore, accept our offering!


PRAYER OF SACRIFICE

AERIES: O Noble, Ancient, and Shining Ones! Wine, nectar, and aromatics we have offered to your shrines. Offerings have we made through the Well, the Fire, and the Tree. Kindreds! All this we give to you in the name of hospitality. May our piety increase your magic! May our courage increase your power! And may our fertile spirits show the world your abundance! Mighty Kindreds, accept our sacrifices!

ALL: Mighty Kindreds, accept our sacrifices!


PIACULAR OFFERING

ANGELA: If out of ignorance or faulty memory, if we for any reason have offended you, O Kindreds of our people, hear us now. Accept this offering as recompense, and know our hearts and our intents are pure.

Make an offering of olive oil.


OMEN

AERIES: Seer, what blessings and wisdom do the Kindreds offer us in return?

Seer (MARTEN) takes the omen.




RECEIVING THE BLESSINGS

ANGELA: Children of the Earth, all our offerings, all we have done so far in our rite, have led to this moment. We have made offerings to the Powers, and now we shall ask for their blessings in return. Visualize the blessings that each of you desire and need from the Kindreds. See them clearly in your mind’s eye. And when we ask for the Waters of Life, when we say, “Give us the Waters of Life,” see the blessing you desire descend into the upraised cup like a mist, filling the cup. And when we drink of the Waters, you will receive your blessing. So close your eyes and take a moment to visualize your needs, and the needs of our Grove, that we may all receive the blessings we desire.

Allow time for the folk to meditate on their needs and desires.

Altar Keeper pours Kykeon into the cup and hands it to the ANGELA.

ANGELA: O Ancient, Noble, and Shining Ones! We have offered to you, so we ask for your gifts in return.

Angela holds high the Waters.

AERIES: Hallow these Waters! Bless our lives with magic, power, and abundance as we drink of your essence. Kindreds, we ask you, give us the Waters of Life!

ALL: Give us the Waters of Life!

AERIES: Behold the Waters of Life! Children of the Earth, drink the Waters of Life!

BARDS: Pour the Waters, followed by instrumental and percussion.


AFFIRMATION OF THE BLESSING

ANGELA: We have drunk of the Waters of Life! The Powers have given us true and wondrous blessings. We affirm the gifts of our Kindreds and acknowledge their power in our lives.

Children of the Earth, do you accept the gifts of the Gods and Goddesses?

ALL: We do!



WORKING

Guided Mother Wound Meditation, with pomegranate seeds and the burning of the child name on paper.

Music: Healing Chant

Heal this spirit, heal this soul.
Come to me, summer’s evening,
Mind and body shall be whole.

She of the ceasing sunlight
Seal this wound that pain may cease.
Mistress of harvest twilight,
Hold us fast and bring us peace.

THANKING AND CLOSING THE GATES

AERIES: The Mighty Ones have blessed us! And now, before we depart, we will give thanks to those who have aided us in this rite tonight. To Demeter and Kore, Kharin soi ekhomen! [KHAH-rin soy EH-khoh-men]  We thank you!

ALL: We thank you!

ANGELA: Mighty Gods and Goddesses, noble Spirits of the Land, and beloved Ancestors, our Kindreds, to you we say Kharin soi ekhomen! [KHAH-rin soy EH-khoh-men]  We thank you!

ALL: We thank you!

INSPIRATION: Iambe [ee-AHM-bay], for your inspiration and strength of voice and song, we say Kharin soi ekhomen! [KHAH-rin soy EH-khoh-men]  We thank you!

ALL: We thank you!

WARRIOR: Fierce Athena, Guardian of our rite, for your watching and warding, through magic and arms, we say Kharin soi ekhomen! [KHAH-rin soy EH-khoh-men]  We thank you!

ALL: We thank you!

GATEKEEPER: Hecate, Keeper of the keys to the Cosmos and guardian of the crossroads, we say Kharin soi ekhomen! [KHAH-rin soy EH-khoh-men]   We thank you!

ALL: We thank you!

GATEKEEPER: Once more we ask you to join your magic with ours and aid us as we close the gates. Now let the Fire be flame, let the Well be water, and let the Omphalos [AHM-fuh-lohs] be but the very heart of us all that we carry forth from this place. Let all be as it was before. Let the Gates be closed!

ALL: Let the Gates be closed!


THANKING THE EARTH MOTHER

EARTH MOTHER: Earth Mother, we give you all remaining blessings of the cup, for what comes from the Earth may return to the Earth.

Pour remaining Waters to the ground.

O Earth Mother, for upholding the world and granting us your blessings, we say Kharin soi ekhomen! [KHAH-rin soy EH-khoh-men]   We thank you!

ALL: We thank you!

Thanking the Hearth Goddess Hestia

ANGELA: Let us give final honor to the hearth goddess, Hestia.

HESTIA: Hestia, first and last!
Make an offering of wine.

Hestia, for keeping watch over our hearth, and for lighting our paths to our community and our grove, we say Kharin soi ekhomen! [KHAH-rin soy EH-khoh-men]  We thank you!

ALL: We thank you!




CLOSING THE RITE

AERIES: Once again, we have continued our traditions, honoring the Shining Ones, Ancestors, and Nature Spirits, just as in times of old. We have made offerings to our Kindreds and received their blessings in return. But great energy has been raised within us this night! Let us be grounded once more.

Angela rings the chimes three times.
Walk with wisdom, Children of the Earth. The rite is ended!
