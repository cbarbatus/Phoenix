Statement of Purpose
Tweak wording if easier/to what feels more natural if desired— these are just bones.

 

We gather on holy Samhain, the New Year, to travel the Otherworld as it joins with our world most intensely on this night. We would honor our mighty dead, the Great Raven Queen Morrigan, and the regenerative warrior-healer Dagda. Whether we visit the Otherworld to grieve our beloved dead or simply to walk the path of living magic, let us join to gather as one people and learn what insight and wisdom may be gleaned from the Realm of Spirit on Samhain Eve. Tonight is the Celtic New Year, so let us not only honor our dead, but unburden ourselves and bury the dead weight that we have been carrying with us. To bury our dead weight on this sacred night of death would be a night of tremendous power to do so, and to go into the new year unburdened and blessed. I invite you all to feel the courage of your ancestors, your gods and your guides, and be who you were born to be unburdened from this night forward. Biodh se amlaidh.

 

But this is not only a night of death: this is a night of spirit, and this is our FIRST SAMHAIN SINCE 2019! Let’s bring the spirit of magic to life with our bards!
