Statement Of Purpose


HEATHER: We gather here to celebrate Imbolc, the time before the birth of lambs when the ewes come into their milk. For some, Imbolc remains a day frozen in the grip of winter, when half the stores are gone, and the land is still bleak and quiet. But here, in this land, we recognize the stirring of life beneath our feet, as winter begins to depart, as the rivers flood, as the days begin to lengthen, as the first buds appear on the trees, and tulips push through the cold soil to greet the sun.
 
NATASHA: We are here to offer worship to the triple goddess Brighid: She who protects, heals, and inspires. Renewal begins for mother earth; seeds take root and come to flower. As the light strengthens and begins to awaken the land, let it be so for us as well; let us clear away the old, dead leaves of winter and prepare our soil to be planted anew, fresh and clear with intention and ripe with purpose.
We will perform our first bit of magick with Millet Bird Seed tonight. We’ll make wishes on the seeds to be carried to the Gods and Goddesses as you feed your local birds. Those seeds that don’t get eaten may sprout, blossoming into fruition, so it’s a win/win!
 
HEATHER: As our ancestors did, so do we now: we are here to honor the Old Bargain between mortals and the Kindreds. So let us join together as one folk to make our offerings in joy and reverence.

NATASHA: Children of the earth, it is time to meet the Goddess Brighid.
 
Heather: 
Stand, sit, or lie down in a comfortable position. Take in a deep breath, allowing the element of air to cleanse you of your troubles of the day. Squeeze, every muscle in your body, hold it, now release. Take a second breath, allowing yourself to release any tension in your body. Now take a third breath, and see yourself in a beautiful field, so plush, and green. There are cows mooing in the distance. It’s a beautiful night, the moon is full and bright. 

You walk a little and discover that you’re at a doorway to a great brightly lit house. You knock, and the door opens on its own. You see a beautiful redhead standing before you, smiling.  She’s surrounded by many beautiful things:  a warm fiery cauldron, a well inside her house, no lie. She has smithing tools, musical instruments, she has an immense apothecary with items that heal, and do great works of magic. It’s Brigid, Bride to some, St. Brigid to others a Goddess of many names, worshiped by many different people.

“Welcome”, she says. “I’m glad you’re here. You are on a journey, and I would like to help you along your path. What would you like to know, that will help you along your path. Ask me anything. I will speak to you more about this in your dreams. Listen for me, and I’ll be there.”

“What tool would you like to have to help you along your journey?” She gives it to you.
How would you like to be healed, to strengthen you on your journey? It is done.

“What is it, that you would like to achieve this season, that will put you further along in your journey? Close your eyes and see it, clearly, what it looks like, what it feels like. It is yours. As above, so below! If you need me, for protection, or further insight, or healing, if you need me call on me, and I will be there.”

You open your eyes. The house is gone. The cows are gone. There is only the moon, and yet, you still know what she told you, you are still healed, and you still have the tool she gave you, and your success is imminent. Return to this room, bringing all of this with you.
