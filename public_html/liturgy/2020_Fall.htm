INITIATING THE RITE

Paul: Children of the earth! Welcome to the Alban Elfed ritual of Raven’s Cry Grove, ADF! Release all the cares from your mundane life. Breathe deeply, and allow your mind to be still. All thoughts outside of this time are released. You are here, spiraling ever forward into the present.


Lisa: Feel the pull of our sacred space, calling you, whispering on the Winds. Here, in our Nemeton, we create our sacred realities. We can be all that the Kindreds believe us to be. Purification allows us to transform into our sacred selves. Please come forward, be purified by walking under the arch of pomegranate branches and passing between flames. Attune yourself to mythic time. Let go and cross the divide.

If there is instrumental accompaniment for the entrance chant, the leaders should have previously confirmed the key/starting note with the musicians, and should use a pitchpipe or other means to start singing the chant in the same key as the musicians. The leaders now start singing the entrance chant and lead the folk in the procession.


Form the Procession –two druids form an arch of pomegranate branches.  Everyone walks under and passes between flames as they exit the arch.


Processional Chant

Come we now as a people
To gather at the sacred well.
Come we now as a people
Together in the warmth and the light of the flame. (Repeats until finished)

Paul:
By the light of the fire and might of the water we find our balance. May this grove be made whole and holy.
Pour water around the fire.

Lisa: Here we stand now, gathered at the threshold. Warrior, call a guardian for our rite!


Protector: [Standing in the North]

“Gwenllian ferch Gruffydd [Gwen-thian Vairk Griffuth], daughter of princes,
Leader of the Patriotic Revolt,
Beautiful, independent and mighty.
In the defense of your people, you raised an army,
With your sword in hand,
You inspired your people to rise up against invaders;
We ask you now to also keep our grove safe and whole.
Repel all those who do not support or uphold our work,
And celebrate with us as we give thanks for the harvest.
We offer you wine,
In thanks for your vigil.

[Offering of Wine]

Derbyn ein Offrwm! /DER-bin ain OFF-rum/ Gwenllian [Gwen-thian], accept our offering!

Bydded Felly. /BUTH-ed FESS-tee/

Lisa: Here we mark our threshold, opening a roadway to the sacred center. Warrior, mark now this space, so that the Outdwellers may not enter.

Warrior draws a line at the entrance with a sword, staff or stick.

Protector: Gwenllian [Gwen-thian], we ask you to guard this threshold!

Lisa: Children of the earth, as we prepare to make our offering to the Outdwellers, avert your eyes so as not to form a relationship with them. Search your own hearts and set aside any negativity or ill feelings that may have come with you into this space. This is a celebration of Love, and all that we carry into the Nemeton today will flower. Take only that which you wish to root, grow and flourish.

Warrior, make offering and appease the Outdwellers who do not support our ways!


Protector: Spirits of this place and afar, those of you who do not uphold our ways or would seek to do us harm or mischief. You who are called Corrannyeid /CORR-an-nee-ite/, we acknowledge the differences between us and ask that you neither hinder nor prevent us from honoring the Kindreds and receiving their blessings.

Take these spirits [Whiskey Offering] as a gift from us, and by doing so acknowledge our request and leave us in peace for our rite.
Bydded Felly. /BUTH-ed FESS-tee/



Paul rings chime 3x3 saying...
Ancestors! (chime 3x)
Nature Spirits! (chime 3x)
Shining Ones! (chime 3x)
 
We have crossed the threshold into sacred space.  Please reconnect with the sacred cosmos as we prepare to open the gates to the otherworlds, the place where the kindreds will reveal themselves once more.


HONORING THE EARTH MOTHER


Paul: Let us begin by honoring the Earth, our home.

Earth Mother:
“Modron, mother of Mabon  /MAH-bawn/ …
Guardian of the Otherworld.
Protector, Healer, the Earth herself.
You are the ground we walk on,
You provide us the food that we eat,
the trees to build shelter and make fire.
You are all a mother should be,
And we celebrate with your Children this eve.
We give you love, honor and respect.
May the sky not fall,
May the sea not rise,
And may the land remain firm.”

[Make Offering of Grain]

Derbyn ein Offrwm! /DER-bin ain OFF-rum/ Earth Mother, accept our offering!



STATEMENT OF PURPOSE



Lisa: Today we gather on the fulcrum between light and darkness, to honor and worship the Kindreds as one people. We sing their praises as we make our sacrifice, and we seek their blessings in return.


Paul:  Croeso y Pob Bendith /CROY-so uh pob BEN-dith/. Welcome and many blessings. We have gathered today to celebrate the autumn harvest and the balancing forces it brings, for today is Alban Elfed, “Light of the Water.” We come together to celebrate the Earth in her full abundance and to give thanks for what things are ready to be harvested in our own lives. Let us rejoice and give due honor to the powers that have assisted us this year!


Lisa: On this Equinox, as we celebrate the balance of day and night, it is only right that we give honor to both of these forces this evening. Tonight we will call on Beli Mawr [Belly Mowr], the father of the House of Light. We will call also on Llyr [Thleer], the father of the House of Darkness.

Without both dark and light, there can be no balance. Without balance, we would be at the mercy of the forces of chaos. Let us give honor, then, this evening to the chthonic and primal fathers of the gods themselves.


Paul: As our ancestors did and so our children may do: we are here to honor the Old Bargain between mortals and the Kindreds.  So let us join together as one folk to make our offerings in joy and reverence.
 
“Bydded Felly”   /BUTH-ed FESS-tee/



INSPIRATION


Paul: And now let us call for Inspiration, so that we may speak the rite in beauty.

Inspiration:
Cerridwen /KEH-ridh-wen/, shape shifter and cauldron stirrer,
You who magically gave birth to Taliesin /TALL-yes-in/,
The greatest of bards,
Come into our grove now,
We come seeking Awen, divine inspiration.
Guide our rite in the way of truth!

Bydded Felly”   /BUTH-ed FESS-tee/

[Offering of Wine]
Derbyn ein Offrwm! /DER-bin ain OFF-rum/ Cerridwen /KEH-ridh-wen/, accept our offering!



ESTABLISH THE GROUP MIND

Grounding: Two Powers Meditation



RECREATE THE COSMOS



Paul: Children of the earth, let us now re-establish the Sacred Center of the Worlds.

The Three Realms

Lisa:
The waters support and surround us.
The land extends about us.
The sky stretches above us.
And the center burns a living flame.
Let us pray with a good fire.
May all the Kindred bless us.
May our worship be true.
May our actions be just.
May our love be pure.
Blessings and honor and worship to the holy ones.


The Hallows


Paul: Let us sanctify the hallows by which we will make our offering.

Well

Well: Ffynnon Sanctaidd /FUH-non SAINK-taith/, Sacred Well, you hold the waters of Annwn [Ahn-noon]. You are the source of all, and into you all things return once more. You are the well of dark wisdom. You are the blood of the earth. We give you this offering [Silver], and ask that you be in this vessel and in us all.

Ffynnon Sanctaidd, llifa on mewn /FUH-non SAINK-taith THLEE-va own ME-oon/! Sacred Well, flow within us!”

Fire

Fire: Dan Sanctaidd /dan SAINK-taith/, Sacred Fire, you are the holy light of the Shining Ones. You provide us with inspiration and call on us to act with courage and right action. You are the element of controlled chaos, and with your power you can both create and destroy. We give you this offering [Oil], and ask that you be in this vessel and in us all.

Dan Sanctaidd, llosga o’n mewn /dan SAINK-taith THLOWS-ka own ME-oon/! Sacred Fire, burn within us!”

Tree


Tree: Coeden Sanctaidd /COY-ten SAINK-taith/, Sacred Tree, you are the boundary of all worlds and the bridge between Earth and Sky. You are a creature rooted deeply in the earth, stretching far into the heavens. You are our sacred pillar and the center of our grove. We give you this offering [flowers], and ask that you be our sacred center.
Coeden Sanctaidd, tyfa o’n mewn! /COY-ten SAINK-taith TUH-vee own mewn/Sacred Tree, grow within us.”



OPEN THE GATES



Paul: We have sanctified the hallows. Now let us call on a Gate Keeper to bring the worlds together.


Gates:  (using staff to point to hallows)
May this be the mound of Arbeth, where wonders are seen.
May we know the wonders...
We call out to the woman on the white horse, slowly riding,
swiftly riding,
waiting for us to ask her to stay.
Rhiannon [rrhee an nown]!
We ask you to lead us to the Otherworld,
to share with us the feast of the Shining ones,
to sing their praises, and drink of their cup. 

Derbyn ein offrwm! /DER-bin ain OFF-rum/ Rhiannon, accept our offering!

[Birdseed and Carrots Offering]

Rhiannon [rrhee an nown], Gate Keeper, share your magic with ours as we open the ways. Let us together make the well a gate to the worlds below! Let us together make the fire a gate to the worlds above! Let us together make the tree the crossroads of all the worlds!

Agor y gatiau /ah-GORR uh GAH-tyah/! Let the Gates be opened!
Bydded Felly /BUTH-ed FESS-tee/



INVITING THE THREE KINDREDS


Honoring and Invoking the Powers


Paul: Children of the earth! So that we may honor them, make now our offerings to the Kindreds: the Ancestors, the Nature Spirits and the Shining Ones.

Ancestors


Ancestors:  Hynafiaid /hee-NAH-fee-ait/, Ancestors- we remember you in the blood in our veins, in the pulse of our heart that is the rhythm of all humankind. We revere you for the gifts and the guidance that you provide. It is because you lived that we live. You are the Mighty Ones, and we call on you as our kin. Take this simple offering and join us in our rite.

[Whiskey Offering]

Hynafiaid /hee-NAH-fee-ait/,  derbyn ein offrwm! /DER-bin ain OFF-rum/ Ancestors, accept our offering!


Lisa: Children of the earth, close your eyes, take a moment, and feel the Ancestors as they join us here.

Allow a few moments.

Nature Spirits


Nature Spirits: Ysbrydion Natur /Ees-BREE-dee-own na-tur/ Nature Spirits- you are the noble ones with whom we share this planet, this Earth. You are our kin of fir, fin, feather, and scale. You are of rock, leaf, flower and branch. We call on you, Tylwyth Teg /TELL-with tegg/, fair folk, the faery kin. You who are our allies, you who we rely on for life itself, we call on you in honor. Take this simple offering and join us in our rite.

[Fruit Offering]


Nature Spirits: Ysbrydion Natur, derbyn ein offrwm! /ess-PRETT-ee-own NA-tare, DER-bin ain OFF-rum/! Nature Spirits, accept our offering!

Lisa: Children of the earth, close your eyes, take a moment, and feel the Nature Spirits  as they join us here.

Allow a few moments.


Shining Ones


Deities: Dduwiau /THOO-ee-aye/, Shining Ones, our own Gods and Goddesses. Those of you who are the House of Light and the House of Darkness, and the patrons of all those present today. You are our eldest and wisest kin. You are our greatest of guides, our best of friends, and our most nurturing parents. Where would we be without the graces of the gods? You who are the Shining Ones, we call on you as Elders. Take this simple offering and join us in our rite.

[Oil Offering]
 
Dduwiau, derbyn ein offrwm! /THOO-ee-aye DER-bin ain OFF-rum/ Shining Ones, accept our offering!


Lisa: Children of the earth, close your eyes, take a moment, and feel the Shining Ones as they join us here.

Allow a few moments.



Praise Offerings

Paul: Children of the earth, as the spirit moves you, come forward and make praise offerings to the Kindreds or your Patrons. You may offer at the well, tree or fire. You may sing songs, recite poetry, dance or do whatever would please the Kindreds.

Folk make offerings as they will

Hallows slides
Instrumental Rhiannon


Bards - Bardic Offering –
Vocal Let All Things



KEY OFFERINGS


Main Invocation

Paul:  The Wheel of the Year turns ever forward. We’ve now arrived at the final High Day before beginning a new year at Samhain. As we bid farewell to the warmth and light of summer, let us look back at these months ruled by the sun and give thanks for the gifts and blessings we’ve received. (pause for reflection) Let us now call on The Great,  Beli Mawr [Belly Mowr], to join us in this sacred space and hear our prayers.

Beli Mawr:
Beli Mawr [Belly Mowr], we call on you,
Oh great ancestor!
Shining god of light and fire,
Father of Arianrhod [Ah-RREE-ahn-hrroad] and Caswallawn, [------------]
You are the Father of the House of Light,
At Beltaine you are the Fire of Life-
At Samhain you are the Fire of Death.
May you be for us the Fire of Bounty!
Join us here, in this place of your worship.
Great Father Sun, join us in our rite!

[Frankincense Offering]



Lisa: As we enter into the beginning of the time of darkness, let us acknowledge what we have worked hard to sow. Let us think upon our good deeds while remaining aware of areas in which we may do better in the months ahead. (pause for reflection) Now let us call on Llyr [thleer], the Father of the Children of Darkness to join us in this sacred space and hear our prayers.


Llyr:
Llyr [thleer], we call on you,
Great Father of the Seas,
Dark god of stormy waves and waters,
You who rules the ever-changing seas,
Father of Bran and Branwen,
Father of the House of Darkness.
You whose waters house the mysteries of life and death,
You whose powers are those of chaos-
In this time when Night and Day stand in balance,
May you be for us the waters of abundance.
Join us here in this place of your worship,
Great God of the Sea, join us in our rite!
[Stout Offering]

MAIN OFFERING



Paul: As this day symbolizes the end of the harvest, we will give an offering of grain. Yet we will offer more than just grain. We shall imbue the bread with our own reflections of this year’s bounty and with our prayers of thanks. We will pass this loaf of bread around so that all may lay their hands upon it. Think now on the past year. Think of the joys and the sorrows that it brought. Think of all that succeeded and thrived and all that failed to flourish. Be grateful for the struggles, for they have shaped you and led you to where you are now. You may express your gratitude aloud or silently, as you so choose.

As you do this, think fondly of this rite’s patrons, the representatives of Light and Darkness. They are the balance in our lives.


Bread is passed around. 
Instrumental Let All Things Now Living


Paul: Beli Mawr [Belly Mowr], Llyr [thleer]! Your children have poured their hearts into this offering! May you find the energy contained within to be your nourishment and strength, much as we are strengthened and nourished by your grace!


Paul:  
(Put bread into the fire.)
Great Powers - Derbyn ein offrwm! /DER-been ain HOF-rum/. Accept our offering!
Bydded Felly!        /BUTH-ed FESS-tee/



PRAYER OF SACRIFICE



Lisa:
O Noble, Ancient and Shining Ones!
Incense have we offered to Your shrines!
Offerings have we made through the Well, Fire and Tree!
Kindreds!  All this we give to You in the name of Hospitality.
May our thankful gifts and Courage increase Your Power and magic!
And may our Fertile Spirits show the world Your abundance!
Mighty Kindreds, accept our sacrifices!


Piacular Offering


Paul: All immortal gods whatever be your names:
If out of ignorance or faulty memory,
if something in this ceremony was displeasing to you,
O Kindreds of our people, hear us now.
By this offering of recompense,
We beg forgiveness and make amends for our error
Know our hearts and our intent are pure.

[Oil Offering]



OMEN


Lisa:
Seer, what blessings and wisdom do the Kindreds offer us in return?


Seer: takes the omen


ASK FOR BLESSINGS


Paul: Children of the earth, all of our offerings, all we have done so far in our rite, have led to this moment.  This is the time of magic!  We have made offerings to the Powers, and now we shall ask for their blessings in return.  Visualize the blessings that each of you desire and need from the Kindreds.  See it clearly in your mind’s eye. And when we ask for the Waters of Life, when we say, “Give us the Waters”, see the blessing you desire descend into the upraised cup like a mist, filling the cup.  And when we drink of the Waters, you will receive your blessing.  So close your eyes and take a moment to visualize your needs, and the needs of our Grove, that we may all receive the blessings we desire.
 

Allow time for the people to meditate on needs and desires. Altar Keeper pours mead into the cup and druids hold it.


Paul: We call for the blessing of Beli Mawr [Belly Mowr],. Together we bid…
 Dduwiau, rhowch i ni'r Dyfroedd! [Thoo-ee-aye, row-ook ih near Duff-royth] Shining Ones, give us the waters!
 
All: Shining Ones, give us the waters!
 

Lisa: We call for the blessing of Llyr [thleer]. Together we ask…
Dduwiau, rhowch i ni'r Dyfroedd bywyd [Thoo-ee-aye, row-ook ih near Duff-royth]!  Shining Ones, give us the waters!

All: Shining Ones, give us the waters!

Elevate the Waters.

A pause…
 

Paul: Behold the waters of life!  Children of the earth, drink of the waters of life!
 
 
Song: Pour the Waters one time, then drumming and music etc.



AFFIRMATION OF THE BLESSINGS


Paul: We have drunk of the Waters of Life!  The Powers have given us true and wondrous blessings.  We affirm the gifts of our Kindreds and acknowledge their power in our lives.
 
Children of the earth, do you accept the gifts of the Gods?


All: We do!



THANKING AND CLOSING THE GATES


Paul: We have given to the powers and the powers have given to us. Let us now thank them before they depart.
 

Lisa:  Beli, Lllyr. Our patrons of the balance who we have honored here tonight. Diolch i chi,  /dee-OLKH ee khee/ we thank you!
 

aul:  Allies. Diolch i chi,  /dee-OLKH ee khee/ we thank you!
 

Lisa: Gods and Goddesses of elder days; noble Spirits of the Land; mighty Ancestors; our Kindreds, we say, . Diolch i chi, /dee-OLKH ee khee/ we thank you!
 

Inspiration:  Cerridwen. Keeper of the cauldron of inspiration. For inspiring us, Diolch i chi, /dee-OLKH ee khee/ we thank you!
 

Protector:  Gwenllian [Gwen-thian]. Courageous noble. For watching over us, Diolch i chi, /dee-OLKH ee khee/ we thank you!
 
Closing the Gates


Gates:  Rhiannon. Magical, divine, mother of Pryderi, and Sovereign Queen. For aiding us in keeping the ways open during our rite, we say to you, Diolch i chi /dee-OLKH ee khee/, we thank you!
 
And now join your magic with ours once more.
Let us together make this well but water.
Let us together make this fire but flame.
Let us together make this sacred tree no more than the living creature that it is.
Cau'r gatiau! /kire GAH-tyah/ Let the gates be closed.



THANKING THE EARTH MOTHER


EarthMother:  Modron. Earth Mother. We return to you all blessing gone unused. May you drink deep and grow stronger by doing so. For always holding firm beneath us, Diolch i chi, /dee-OLKH ee khee/ we thank you!


Protector: And now that the gates have been closed… Outdwellers, for accepting our terms and leaving us in peace during our rite, we thank you and return this space to you until we may have need of it again. Diolch i chi /dee-OLKH ee khee/, we thank you.


CLOSING THE RITE



Lisa:  Once again, we have continued in our traditions, honoring the Gods,
Ancestors and Nature Spirits, just as in times of old.  We have made offerings to our Kindreds and received their blessings in return.
 
But great energy has been raised within us this day!  Let us be grounded once more.

[Grounding of excess energies.]
 

Paul: Let us end as we began. (Chime 3x3)
 

Paul:  Children of the earth, Pob Bendith /pob BEN-dith/. Many Blessings. The rite has ended. Bydded felly /BUTH-ed FESS-tee/.

Bards: closing song
