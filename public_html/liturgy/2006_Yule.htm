Welsh Midwinter Ritual

Raven’s Cry Grove, ADF

Held January 6, 2007

Written By Coedwig

 

 

I. Establishing the Grove

 

PD Begins with chime 3x3

 

Call for Protector

Druid: “Lleu Llaw Gyffes, Lleu of the deft arm. With your strength and skill you were named. With your cunning, you were armed. With the magic of your great uncle, you were wed. Brother of Dylan, foster son of Gwydion. We ask you to come into the grove and protect it and those within as we do our work this evening. We offer you ale [ale offering] , so that you do not thirst, and bread, [bread offering] so that you do not hunger as you keep your vigil.”

“Derbyn ein hoffrwm! Accept our offering!”

“Bydded Felly.”

 

 

Procession- Song to be figured out by Lisa and Russell. Based on “Mothers and Fathers of Old”.  Censing and Aspersing done here too.

 

Opening Statement- PD

 

“  Croeso y Pob Bendith. Welcome and many blessings. We have come together to honor the powers, especially Taliesin, Bard of Wales. May we be together tonight with love and warmth in our hearts in this time of rest.

 

Earth Mother

 

PD: “ We will begin by honoring the Earth, our home.”

 

Druid: " O beloved Mother, Great spirit of the earth, our home. You who are the sea bordering us, the mountains surrounding us, the sky above us. It is from you that all life as we know it springs forth. Tonight, we call on you as Don, Mother Goddess of Wales and ask that you watch over us this night and bless this rite. We give you this offering of warm cider with so much love and gratitude in our hearts.

 

Goddess Don, Earth Mother, derbyn ein hoffrwm, accept our offering!”

 

Inspiration

 

PD: “ And now we call for inspiration, so that we may speak the rite in beauty”

 

Druid:  “ Ceridwen, keeper of the cauldron, amen, source of awen, the fiery waters of inspiration. Changer of shape, bane of Gwion, mother of Taliesin; Come into our grove now and kindle our heads with the brightness of your inspiration so that our words ring sweet in the ears of the powers. Make our voices strong, our minds clear, our bodies able. Guide our rite in the way of truth.

 

Bydded Felly!”

 

II. Opening of the Grove

 

Druid: Grove Attunement- Two powers centering

 

The Hallows

 

PD: “ We now sanctify the hallows by which we make offering.”

 

Well

Druid:  “ May the waters of the earth be in this vessel; the rivers, streams, lakes, oceans and the waters of darkness that flow under the earth. May the dark realms of the beloved ancestors and cythonic gods and spirits be brought here and channeled through this vessel. We make our call with bright silver so that these waters may be a shrine for our honoring of the ancestors.

 

Ffynnon Sanctaidd, llifa o’n mewn! Sacred Well, flow within us!”

 

 

Fire

Druid: “ May the sacred fires kindled by our ancestors be remembered this night. May this flame be for us the fires of the hearth, of love, passion, and inspiration; the bright heat of the sun and the cool light of the stars and moon, and the bright places where the shining and celestial gods and spirits live. May all those things be brought here and channeled through this flame. We make our call with sweet smelling oil so that this flame may be a shrine for our praising of the gods .

 

Dan Sanctaidd, llosga o’n mewn! Sacred Fire, burn within us!”

 

Tree

Druid: “ May this simple staff of wood be for us a tree - the physical representation of the connectedness of the world. Through seeing its branches reaching high and its roots digging deep may we also span the heights and depths. May the green places where the gods and goddesses or nature and nature spirits reside be brought here through this sacred tree. We give it water so that it may drink deep, and incense so that it may breath sweetly. May it be now a shrine for our worshiping of the spirits of all the worlds.

 

Coeden Sanctaidd, tyfa o’n mewn! Sacred Tree, grow within us.”

 

 

 

 

Opening the Gates

 

PD: “ We have sanctified the hallows for our worship. Now let us call on the Gate Keeper to bring the worlds together.”

 

Druid: “ Gwyn ap Nudd, Lord of the Otherworld. Out of the deep unknown, we call you. Lord of Annwfn. Out of the obscure mist, we call you. Ruler of the Tylwyth Teg, the fair folk, out of the dark woods we call you. You who lead the dead to your court under the hill during your nightly rides across the sky, we call on you to make the way ready for us. Come to us, with your blackened face and dapple-gray horse, with your ghostly hounds trailing behind you. Ward us, so that we walk the ways in safety.

 

Derbyn ein hoffrwm! Accept our offering and hear our call!

 

Share your magic with ours as we open the ways.

Let us, together, make the well a gate to the homes of the ancestors!

Let us, together, make the fire a gate to the homes of the gods!

Let us, together, make the tree a gateway to all the spirits of the Earth!

 

Agorer Y Pyrth! Let the Gates be opened!

 

III. Offering to the Powers

 

Honoring and Invoking the Powers

 

PD: “ Let us now call on the kindreds so we may honor them.”

 

Nature Spirits

Druid: “ Ysbrydion Natur, Nature Spirits. Spirits who share this world with us.

Children of the green earth. Animals of fir, fin, feather, and scale. Creatures of leaf, flower, rock, and bark, The Tylwyth Teg and faery of all lands. On this night we give you our respect and ask that you join us at our hearthside. Noble Ones, we give you this offering of fruits and say together:

Nature Spirits, accept our offering!

 

Bydded Felly!

 

Ancestors

Druid: “ Hynafiaid, Ancestors. Those whom we are related to in blood and those that we revere for their gifts to the world. Great warriors, priests, chieftains, bards, and hand workers of the distant past. Grandparents, parents, aunts, uncles, and dear ones of the recent past. On this night we give you our love and ask that you join us at our hearthside. Mighty Ones, we give you this offering of grain and say together:

Ancestors, accept our offering!

 

Bydded Felly!

 

Deities

Druid: “ Dduwiau, gods and goddesses of the House of Don and gods and goddesses of the House of Llyr as well as the patron gods of all those gathered in this place. Eldest and the wisest of spirits, greatest of guides, best of friends, and most nurturing of parents. On this night we give you our worship and ask that you join us at our hearthside. Shining Ones, we give you this offering of whiskey and say together:

Shining Ones, accept our offering!

 

Bydded Felly!

 

Personal Offerings

PD: “ If anyone is called to make offering to their gods and allies, please do so now.”

 

Main Offering

A poem, song, and story is to be offered as well as a chant for all to participate in.

 

 

The Omen

PD: “ We have honored the powers and given to them. It is now time for the flow to turn and the powers to reciprocate. Let us begin by seeing how our offerings have been received and what messages the powers may wish to give us in return.

 

Seer: Take omen

 

Blessing Cup

PD: We are now ready to receive all the blessings the powers may wish to give us.

Druid: We call for the blessing of the Ancient Ones. Together we bid…Dduwiau, rhowch i ni'r Dyfroedd! Shining Ones, give us the waters!

Druid: We join our hearts, that each of us be blessed. Together we call…Dduwiau, rhowch i ni'r Dyfroedd! Shining Ones, give us the waters!

Druid: And as we are blessed, let all the worlds be blessed. Together we pray…Dduwiau, rhowch i ni'r Dyfroedd bywyd! Shining Ones, gives the waters of life!

(All Three) Behold the waters of life!

Pour the Waters to be sung while blessing is passed.

Thanking

PD: “ We have given to the powers and the powers have given to us. Let us now thank them before they depart.”

Druid:  “ Taliesin, Great Bard who we have honored tonight and who has honored us, Diolch i chi, we thank you!

Druid: Shining Ones. Gods and goddesses of all those gathered here. Diolch i chi, we thank you!

Druid: Ancestors. Ancient spirits who came before us, Diolch i chi, we thank you!

Druid: Nature Spirits. Wild ones of the green world and those beyond the mists, Diolch i chi, we thank you!

Druid: Ceridwen. Keeper of the cauldron o+f inspiration, for inspiring us, Diolch i chi, we thank you!

Druid:  Lleu Llaw Gyffes. Lleu of the deft arm, for watching over us, Diolch i chi, we thank you!

Druid:  Earth Mother. We return to you all blessing gone unused. May you drink deep and grow stronger by doing so. For always holding firm beneath us, Diolch i chi, we thank you!

Druid:  Gwyn ap Nudd, Lord of the world beyond. Lord of the Tylwyth Teg. For aiding us in keeping the ways open during our rite, we say to you, Diolch i chi, we thank you!

    And now join your magic with ours once more.

Let us together make this well but water.

Let us together make this fire but flame.

Let us together make this sacred tree no more than the stave of wood before us.

 

Grounding to be done here

 

Let us end as we began. (Chime 3x3)

 

Brothers and Sisters, Pob Bendith. Many Blessings. The rite has ended. Bydded felly.
