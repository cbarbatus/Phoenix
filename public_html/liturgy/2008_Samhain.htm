- Opening Statement and Precedence

 

     PD:  We belong to the gods and the gods belong to us, so let us pray with a good fire.  Let us offer with a full heart, knowing that when we do, we nurture our connection to them and support the very gods themselves.  In remembrance, we strengthen their powers.  In honor, we balance the worlds.  In love, we bring peace and keep the Old Ways alive and present. 

 

     Brothers and sisters, welcome to the womb of the forest, and welcome to this celebration of Samhain, the time when the veils between the worlds are thin, and the way between death and life touches one upon the other in strong magic, magic so strong that all the cultures of the world know to be still and to listen in the winds for their Ancestors voices. 

 

     In the darkness of the grave of endings, new seeds wait to sing their songs.  They wait for the fires of necessity and inspiration to stir them to action.  They wait for our attention as do our Ancestors who care for us and guide us, who ward us and heal us.  Tonight dear Ones, we will pay attention.

 

     For we have come to honor our beloved dead and our unknown Ancestors who shape and bless us.  We have come to celebrate their presence, to dance with them and through them.   We have come, in turn, to offer blessings to the generations yet to come, for we dwell in the village of past and future as the fleshy bridge of immortality. 

 

And so we begin this night by calling upon the keeper of the Crossroads, so that we will walk accompanied by the wisdom of one who knows these roads well.

II. Opening of the Grove

 

 

- Grove Attunement – Two Powers and the mists

 

- Statement of Purpose

 

 PD:  In the slow, glowing nights of autumn, we hear lessons in the stillness and feel close to the earth, accepting its cycles.   Now, at harvest end, as the earth turns within, so we of the Old Ways look to the care of our own roots.   Who are the worthy dead who fertilize our soil?   Do we nurture them as they nurture us?  How we do neglect them in our modern world!

 

     In Africa, the birthplace of humankind, it is said that one can not truly know oneself wholly, unless they one can recite their family line at least seven generations back and tell their stories.  Yet, most of us here are children of immigrants, and we do not know our distant ancestors’ names, their lands, or their stories.   But we do know them - in our faces, in our bodies, in our talents, in our intuition, in what is best in us, and in what is weak in us.  We do know them - for we are they and we strengthen them when we remember them and offer the fruits of our hands and bodies to them. 

 

 So, tonight we have really come to a family reunion and a festival of introductions!    Let us learn to see through the Ancestors eyes, dance in joy with them, and welcome those yet to become our own.

WORKING:  Trance Journey and Ancestor Dance

 

PD:     Masks have been used the world over for transformational, transcendent purposes.  They put us a little off balance and place us in mysterious worlds.  The Ancestors of your bones, of your flesh and blood, will show you how to fashion an ancestral medicine mask, when you need to see through their eyes, when you need to reconnect with the strengths of your roots.  Sustained in our blessings, let us, like Death, turn our focus inward to learn and to pay attention.

 

Trance journey - (Loose guide) Holding the mask in your hands – breath, attunement, creation of mask that all your ancestors show you.  Allow the process working together, what symbols do they add of the past, what do you add of the present, what will be added to the future… what is the same throughout the lines, the power colors, symbols, etc.  How can the powers of the mask hold you rooted in wholeness?

 

     Some of you may find that you are asked or already are the medicine keeper of the bones for your family.  Commit to their charge.  What else will you commit to do to sustain and remember them in the year to come?  Holding fast to that commitment will strengthen you in the coming year, opening you to their protection and guidance. 

 

     When your ancestral mask is fashioned in your magic mind’s eye and the essence of the lesson is shared, keeping your eyes closed, place the mask on your face and feel its power move swiftly through your body, your senses, your feelings, your mind, and your spirit, loosening and dissolving the walls you may find.  Feel the love and power of your mighty Ancestors, those of whom you can be proud.  Rise firm in the honor and acknowledgement of your blood.  Let the essence of your Ancestors swirl and rise strong in you, in the sturdy, sensual power of your calves and thighs, the seductive sway of your hips, the strength of your sinewy arms, and feel your open, racing heart that courses with the entirety of life.  Open now, the eyes of your Ancestors in you.   In this shared ecstasy standing beyond space and time, let us serve Spirit with our bodies as we dance.

Space design is ritual specific: 

 

BEFORE RITUAL -  Three large sugar, spiral sigils on the ground in the center of the circle.  Each of the three hallows will be placed in the center of each spiral. In the center of the three spirals as a whole will be a large bowl of water (Spirit Water).  Altar is toward the back of the circle and draped with a white cloth (purple and black cloths can also complement it). 

 

DURING RITUAL - Crossroads will be drawn during ritual toward the front side of the space, leaving space between it and the spirals for the patron powers sigils drawn also during the rite.

 

For this rite, there is also a need for a full time GROVE SACRIFICER.
