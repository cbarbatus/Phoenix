INITIATING THE RITE
 
Procession and Purification

Kaila: Druids, release the cares of your mundane lives.  Take a deep breath in and allow your mind to be still.  Thoughts outside of this time are released.  You are here spiraling ever forward in the present.
 
Natasha: Feel the pull of our sacred space, calling you, whispering on the wind.  In this space we create our sacred realities.  We can become all the Kindreds believe us to be.  Purification allows us to transform into our sacred selves.  Please come forward, be purified, and attune yourself to mythic time.  Let go and cross the divide.

Folk take their place in the circle.

Ancestors, A shinseara (uh HEEN-sher-un)

Grandmothers and grandfathers, Elders and wise ones, known kith or unnamed kin, all of our beloved dead, we offer you welcome!
Mighty Ancestors, A shinseara we would give you honour and praise, and we ask that you join us here today!

Kindred of precious rock and liquid lava. Kindred of stream and fertile soil, kins of the earth, we offer you welcome!

KEY OFFERINGS
 
Kaila: We ask now that our Grove patron, Lugh and his step mother Tailtiu be with us.  First, we call Tailtiu.

Invocation of Tailtiu:
Mike:

Blessed are you, the well-formed goddess, the daughter of kings, the foster mother of Lugh.  Beautiful Tailtu... In your loving-kindness for humanity, you toiled though you were tired, clearing the forest and the creating the plain. With your divine feminine energy, you labored though you were weary, birthing fields of grains to nourish your people. Your actions insured their lives would continue, though yours could not. It was a sacrifice only a mother could make. But blessed Tailtu it was not in vain, for your children still are present and gather here now to honor you and bring praise.

We return your loving-kindness, sweet Tailtu, and present now a portion of all that you have provided as a symbol of our gratitude and affection. Mother, helper, provider and protector, blessed are you.

(offering of vegetables)

Ever Shining One

Hear me Samildanach, as I call to you on behalf of my people.

Offering by the people:

Kaila:   Druid folk, we will now step forth and bring forth our own personal treasures- that which represents the fruits of your own personal harvest. You may take a corn husk, and reflect upon it that which you yourself are bringing into the Nemeton. What are your personal gifts that you can share with the tribe? When your offering is clear in your mind , you write upon the corn husk and offer it to the fire.  You may also visit the four Grove Treasures. The Spear brings passion and creativity.  The Stone brings ancient wisdom.  The Sword brings purity of intent.  The Cauldron brings abundance. As you are drawn toward one of the Grove treasures, you are invited to go and pay honor to that treasure that you might be granted its boon in return.

 

Druid 1 hands out corn husks to the Folk as they line up near the altar. Pencils are provided (Druid 2) for the Folk to write on the husks.

RECEIVING THE BLESSINGS
 
Kaila:
Druids, all our offerings, all we have done so far in our rite, have led to this moment.  This is magic time!  We have made offerings to the Powers, and now we shall ask for their blessings in return.  Visualize the blessings that each of you desire and need from the Kindreds.  See it clearly in your mind’s eye. And when we ask for the Waters of Life, when we say, “Give us the Waters of Life”, see the blessing you desire descend into the upraised cup like a mist, filling the cup.  Those desired and needs will then be in the cup, and when we drink of the Waters, you will receive your blessing.  So close your eyes and take a moment to visualize your needs, and the needs of our Grove, that we may all receive the blessings we desire.
