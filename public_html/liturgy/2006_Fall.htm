Welsh Fall Equinox Ritual

Raven’s Cry Grove, ADF

Written By Coedwig

 

I. Establishing the Grove

 

PD Begins with chime 3x3

 

Call for Protector

 

(Druid)“Lleu Llaw Gyffes, Lleu of the deft arm. With your strength and skill you were named. With your cunning, you were armed. With the magic of your great uncle, you were wed. Brother of Dylan, foster son of Gwydion.  We ask you to come into the grove and protect it and those within as we do our work this evening. We offer you ale [ale offering] , so that you do not thirst, and bread, [bread offering]  so that you do not hunger as you keep your vigil.”

 

“Derbyn ein hoffrwm!  [der-been ayn hof-room]  Accept our offering!”

 

“Bydded Felly.”  [buth-ed veck-lee]

 

Treating with the Outdwellers

 

(Druid) “ Spirits of this place and afar. Those who do not follow our ways, but follow another way. You, who were known to our gods as the Corannyeid, we respect the differences we share and ask that you neither hinder the energies we raise this night nor prevent us from honoring the Kindreds and receiving their blessings.

 

“ Take this ale [ale offering] as a gift from us, and by doing so acknowledge our request and leave us in peace for this rite.

 

“Bydded Felly”   [buth-ed veck-lee]

 

Procession- “We Approach the Sacred Grove”.

Censing and Aspersing of participants

 

Opening Statement- PD

 

“  Croeso y Pob Bendith. Welcome and many blessings. We are here to celebrate the day of Alban Elfed, the Light of Autumn. In the days of our ancestors, this day would signal the end of the first harvest. They would rejoice and give prayers of thanks to our gods for all the blessings they had received. Now, in rememberence of those days, we too give due honor to the powers for all they have assisted us with this year.

 

This day is also the Equinox, when day and night, light and dark are balanced and present in the world in equal force. It is only right that we honor those forces on this evening. Tonight we will call on Beli Mawr and Don; the father and mother of the House of Light. And we will call on Llyr and Penarddun, the father and mother of the House of Darkness.

 

Without them, there would be no balance. Without them, we would be at the mercy of the forces of chaos. They are the fathers and mothers of the gods. Let us honor them this night.”

 

Earth Mother

 

PD: “ Let us begin by honoring the Earth, our home.”

 

 

(Druid)" Spirit of this world. You who are the ground we walk on, build our homes on and sleep on. You who are the sea bordering us, the mountains surrounding us, the sky above us. You are everything a mother should be. We give you our love, honor and respect, Goddess that is this world. May the sky not fall, the sea not rise, and the land hold firm.

 

[grain offering]

 

Derbyn ein Hoffrwm!  [der-been ayn hof-room] Earth Mother, accept our offering!”

 

 

Inspiration  

 

PD: “ And now let us call for inspiration, so that we may speak the rite in beauty”

 

(Druid) “ Ceridwen, changer of shape, bane of Gwion, mother of Taliesin; Come into our grove now with your magic cauldron, the source of awen, the fiery waters of inspiration. Be here with us and fill our heads with your magic so that our words ring sweet in the ears of the powers. Make our voices strong, our minds clear, our bodies able. Guide our rite in the way of truth.

 

[red wine offering]

 

Bydded Felly!”   [buth-ed veck-lee]

 

II. Opening of the Grove

 

Grove Attunement- Two powers centering

 

The Hallows

 

PD: “ Let us sanctify the hallows by which we make offering.”

 

Well

(Druid) “ Ffynnon Sanctaidd, Sacred well. Waters of Annwfn. Portal to the worlds below. Source of all. Well of dark wisdom. Blood of the earth. We make our call with bright silver [silver offering] and ask that you be in this vessel and in us.

 

Ffynnon Sanctaidd, llifa o’n mewn! Sacred Well, flow within us!”

 

 

Fire

(Druid) “ Dan Sanctaidd [Sank-tayth], Sacred Fire. Holy light of the shining ones. Embodiment of inspiration. Fire in the head. Element of controlled chaos. You are capable of both creating and destroying. Bringing both life and death. We make our call to you with sweet smelling oil [oil offering]  and ask that you be in this vessel and in us.

 

Dan Sanctaidd [Sank-tayth], llosga o’n mewn [lowss-ka own me-oon]! Sacred Fire, burn within us!”

 

Tree

(Druid) “ Coeden Sanctaidd [Coy-den Sank-tayth], Sacred Tree. Boundary of all worlds. Creature rooted deep and crowned high. Sacred Pillar. Carry us with you to your depths and raise us up to your heights. Be for us the center of the grove, the hub of the wheel, connecting us to all the worlds. We make our call to you with sacred water and sacred smoke and ask that you be the center of this place.  [insense and water offering]

 

Coeden Sanctaidd [Coy-den Sank-tayth], tyfa o’n mewn! [tuv-a own me-oon]  Sacred Tree, grow within us.”

 

Opening the Gates

 

PD: “ We have sanctified the hallows. Now let us call on the Gate Keeper to bring the worlds together.”

 

(Druid) “ Gwyn ap Nudd, Lord of the Otherworld. Out of the deep unknown, we call you. Lord of Annwfn. Out of the obscure mist, we call you. Ruler of the Tylwyth Teg, the fair folk, out of the dark woods we call you. You who lead the dead to your court under the hill during your nightly rides across the sky, we call on you to make the way ready for us. Come to us, with your blackened face and dapple-gray horse, with your ghostly hounds trailing behind you. Ward us, so that we walk the ways in safety.

 

Derbyn ein hoffrwm! Accept our offering and hear our call!  [offering]

 

Share your magic with ours as we open the ways.

Let us, together, make the well a gate to the worlds below!

Let us, together, make the fire a gate to the worlds above!

Let us, together, make the tree the crossroads of all the worlds!

 

Agorer Y Pyrth! Let the Gates be opened!

 

 

III. Offering to the Powers

 

Honoring and Invoking the Powers

 

PD: “ Let us now call on the kindreds so we may honor them.”

 

Nature Spirits

(Druid) “ Ysbrydion Natur, Nature Spirits. Spirits with whom we share this world. The animals of fir, fin, feather, and scale. Of rock, leaf, flower, and branch. Tylwyth Teg, fair folk, and faery kin. You who are the Noble Ones, we call on you as our allies. Take this simple offering and join us in our rite. 

 

[offering]

 

Ysbrydion Natur, derbyn ein hoffrwm! [der-been ayn hof-room] Nature Spirits, accept our offering!

 

Ancestors

(Druid) “ Hynafiaid, Ancestors. Those whom we are related to in blood and those that we revere for their gifts to the world. It is because you lived that we live. Great warriors, priests, chieftains, and laborers of the distant past. Grandparents, parents, aunts, uncles, and dear ones of the recent past. You who are the Mighty Ones, we call on you as our kin. Take this simple offering and join us in our rite. 

 

[offering]

 

Hynafiaid, derbyn ein hoffrwm! [der-been ayn hof-room] Ancestors, accept our offering!

 

Deities

(Druid)  “ Dduwiau [Thoo-ee-aye], Our gods and goddesses. Those of the House of light, the House of Darkness, and the patrons of all present here. You are the eldest and the wisest. You are our greatest of guides, best of friends, and most nurturing of parents. Where would we be without the graces of the gods? You who are the Shining Ones, we call on you as our elders. Take this simple offering and join us in our rite.

 

[alcohol offering]

 

Dduwiau [Thoo-ee-aye], derbyn ein hoffrwm! [der-been ayn hof-room] Shining Ones, accept our offering!

 

Main Invocation

 

PD: “ This is the last high day before the beginning of the New Year. The last high day before the time of darkness begins again. Before the cycle begins again. A time of balance. This is the time to give thanks for this year’s cycle, and for this year’s gifts. So let us now call on the Patron Powers of this rite so that they may hear our prayers.

 

Let us begin by calling on Beli Mawr and Don, the father and mother of the children of Light.”

 

 

(Druid) Beli Mawr. Hear us now, Bright One. Fire god. God of light. You are the fire of the year. At Beltaine you are the Fire of Life; at Samhain you are the Fire of Death. At this time of the harvest, burn brightly in our hearts and be for us the Fire of Bounty! Cast your flashing, fiery eyes upon those that call to you now and enter our place of worship. Great god, crowned with fire, join us in our rite!

 

(Druid)Don, mother goddess, earth goddess. Hear us now, source womb of all life! You have existed from eternity and exist still as the background principle of all realities. You are ruler of the earth and the waters under the earth that spring up as the rivers, lakes, and sacred wells. Great goddess cloaked in a mantle of stars, be for us the substance of growth and bounty and join us in our rite!

 

(PD): “ Let us now call on Llyr and Penarddun, the father and mother of the children of Darkness”

 

(Druid)Llyr. God of the sea. God of the dark waters. Hear us, Lord of the kingdoms under the waves. You are the ruler of the perilous seas that surround our lands.

Your oceans bring life and death to those that explore its surfaces and depths. They are the waters of chaos. At this time of the harvest, be for us the waters of abundance. Great god of the sea, whose eyes are like that of an eagle’s, join us in our rite!

 

(Druid) Penarddun. Hear us now, daughter of the earth goddess and fire god, wife of the sea god. You are born of the light and married to the darkness. Both aspects of light and dark reside within you. You are the High Goddess, goddess of the headlands. At this time, when light and darkness are of equal length and strength, be for us the principal of Balance. Great goddess, standing proud, join us in our rite!

 

Personal Offerings

PD: “ Now that we have called all the powers to our fireside, if anyone is called to make personal offering, please do so now.”

 

Main Offering

 

PD: “ As this day symbolizes the end of the harvest, we will offer up grain as we did at the beginning of the harvest. But we will not offer just simple grain. We will imbue it with our own reflections of this year’s bounty and with our prayers of thanks. We will pass this loaf of bread around so that all may lay their hands upon it. Think of this past year. Think of the joys and sorrows. Of your successes and failures. Be grateful for the struggles that have shaped you into your present form. Be grateful for the experiences that have allowed you to grow.

 

As you do this think fondly of this rite’s patrons, the representatives of Earth, Sea, and Sky. Of light and dark. Below, above, and middle. They are the balance in our lives…

 

(Bread is passed around. Song played here?)

 

Beli, Don, Llyr, Penarddun! Your children have poured their hearts into this offering! May you find the energy contained within to be your nourishment and strength, much as we are strengthened and nourished by your grace!

 

Great Powers… Derbyn ein haberth. Accept our sacrifice!

Bydded Felly!    [buth-ed veck-lee]

 

The Omen

PD: “ We have honored the powers and given to them. It is now time for the flow to turn and the powers to reciprocate. Let us begin by seeing how our offerings have been received and what messages the powers may wish to give us in return.

 

(Druid) reads omen

 

Blessing Cup

 

PD: We are now ready to receive all the blessings the powers may wish to give us.

 

(Druid)We call for the blessing of the Ancient Ones. Together we bid…

 Dduwiau, rhowch i ni'r Dyfroedd! [Thoo-ee-aye, row-ook ih near Duff-royth] Shining Ones, give us the waters!

 

(Druid) We join our hearts, that each of us be blessed. Together we call…

Dduwiau, rhowch i ni'r Dyfroedd [Thoo-ee-aye, row-ook ih near Duff-royth]! Shining Ones, give us the waters!

 

(Druid) And as we are blessed, let all the worlds be blessed. Together we pray…

Dduwiau, rhowch i ni'r Dyfroedd bywyd! Shining Ones, gives the waters of life!

 

(All Three) Behold the waters of life!

 

Thanking

PD: “ We have given to the powers and the powers have given to us. Let us now thank them before they depart.”

 

(Druid) “ Beli, Don, Llyr, Penarddun. Great parents who we have honored tonight. Diolch i chi,  [di-olch ee hee] we thank you!

 

(Druid) Shining Ones. Gods and goddesses of all those gathered here. Diolch i chi, [di-olch ee hee]we thank you!

 

(Druid) Ancestors. Ancient spirits who came before us. Diolch i chi, [di-olch ee hee]we thank you!

 

(Druid)Nature Spirits. Wild ones of the green world and those beyond the mists. Diolch i chi, [di-olch ee hee]we thank you!

 

(Druid)Ceridwen. Keeper of the cauldron of inspiration. For inspiring us, Diolch i chi, [di-olch ee hee]we thank you!

 

(Druid) Lleu Llaw Gyffes. Lleu of the deft arm. For watching over us, Diolch i chi, [di-olch ee hee]we thank you!

 

(Druid) Earth Mother. We return to you all blessing gone unused. May you drink deep and grow stronger by doing so. For always holding firm beneath us, Diolch i chi, [di-olch ee hee]we thank you!

 

[Grounding of excess energies.]

 

(Druid) Gwyn ap Nudd, Lord of the world beyond. Lord of the Tylwyth Teg. For aiding us in keeping the ways open during our rite, we say to you, Diolch i chi, we thank you!

 

And now join your magic with ours once more.

Let us together make this well but water.

Let us together make this fire but flame.

Let us together make this sacred tree no more than the living creature that it is.

Caeed i Pyrth! Let the gates now be closed!

 

PD: And now that the gates have been closed… Outdwellers, for accepting our terms and leaving us in peace during our rite, we thank you and return this space to you until we may have need of it again. Diolch i chi, we thank you.

 

Let us end as we began. (Chime 3x3)

 

Brothers and Sisters, Pob Bendith. Many Blessings. The rite has ended. Bydded felly.    [buth-ed veck-lee]
