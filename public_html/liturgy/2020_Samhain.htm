Guardian

 

Heather: The days grow shorter, and night comes fast upon us now. In this time of darkness we are here to welcome the last harvest, to enjoy the final fruits of our labor before the coming Winter. In elder days, the Folk knew that spirits were about this night, and they made offerings that the sprites might be gentle. They knew that on this night the Dead are with us and must be given their due, lest they reward our arrogance with mischief. So, on this night, let us remember our Ancestors and all our Honored Dead, let us celebrate with the Nature Spirits around us, and let us honor the Shining Ones, so we may enjoy the Kindreds’ blessings through the darkness of Winter.
