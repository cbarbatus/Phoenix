Statement of Purpose

On Lughnasadh, we remember capability in the fullest sense as a sacred duty. We remember that family is more than blood, but those we choose and who choose us. And we start to accept the turning of the wheel from light to dark. Instead of running from death and pain, we accept it as inevitable— we remember the full wheel of the year is needed for life to be balanced and whole.

 

ReCreate the Cosmos

Fire, Well, Tree: script TBD, as I am inviting those who feel called to do this from the heart. These parts are not complex: if you’ve done rite with us before, you know that anything that achieves the effect of “mighty X, we honor you, fire connect us to the gods, well connect us to the Otherworld/ancestors, Tree connect us to the nature spirits and be the World Tree between the worlds” etc etc— if you understand the actual FUNCTION and PURPOSE of what is being said, evoking is easy. Trust yourself, feel the connection: once it’s felt, you’re already there. When in doubt, say something easily repeatable with “biodh se amlaidh.”

Working:

Not traditional games, but fun + cooperative energy-raising

 

First “game”: Lifting

Hot potato with dumbbells: I can do a quick demonstration of how to curl, then whoever interested in competing steps up to complete as many curls as possible within 1 round of crowd chanting:

 

Fire well and sacred tree, flame and flow and grow in me.

 

For the chanting, those doing the Kindreds and Fire Well and Tree will be directly surrounding the fire, linking hands, chanting the loudest. Embody your thing, and lead the crowd in this chant!

 

Once the interested competitors have all done, we move into the next: take all the energy built during this portion, and apply it to call on the Two Powers ourselves.

 

Second game: Dueling Evocations

Paint faces (just foreheads) red or gold for Morrigan & Lugh as prep before dueling evocations

 

Dueling evocations: folks can choose to chant for, in short, chaos or order. Whichever chant ends up loudest/most stirring wins, and sets the tone for the coming harvest! (I have not chosen a specific chant for both sides yet but I will- will update this section when chosen)

 

Just for example to paint a picture for now (I won’t be using these exactly)— one side for chaos, the other side for order. They chant/yell back and forth. Whichever side raises more energy, will set the tone for the coming harvest.

 

When done/the energy is raised, explain the significance of the grove treasures, and invite the crowd to focus the energy they just raised on one of the treasures that they want to play a larger role in their life as the grovies consecrate the actual objects.

 

Treasure Consecration

Bring forth the treasures around the fire, to be anointed with oil & energy by all grovies.
