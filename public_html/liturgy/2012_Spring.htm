Establish the Group Mind – Two Powers (Story)

Meg:    And now, we will have a story, to center us all.  Let us move together with the actions of the story you are about to hear…

Long, long ago, there was only Darkness, and her name was Danu.  One day, she had a marvelous idea and planted the seed of Inspiration within herself.  Her belly grew and grew, and one day, the waters of life burst forth, and sacred rivers flowed.  Her milk formed the stars above, her waters formed the rivers of life, the banks the sands of time.  In the river, there were rocks, and one of them was Earth.  She held and rocked the Earth and saw on its surface, new life growing, ever-rapidly, and delighted in this. 

                On the Earth, animals and trees thrived, and soon there were people.  The people reached within themselves, and pulled out streams of words, and within these streams, pulled out stones with which to build stories and tales.  They splashed in the rivers of creation and the droplets soared against the sunlight, and rainbows appeared.

                Then one day, Doubt and Fear snuck into the world of people and made themselves at home.  And the people forgot their roots and the magic of the river, forgot that Danu created all, forgot that she is the mother.  Danu was sad, and tried to tell the beings of Earth that they are free and capable of anything they wish.  But Doubt and Fear had put a spell on the people, blocking out Danu’s voice.  She sang the winds that blew the leaves of trees, and smiled sunsets and sunrises.  She turned the flowers’ faces toward the sun, and dreamed green grasses into life after harsh winter’s end.  All the while, she hoped the people would see her and hear her, and break free of Doubt and Fear’s harsh spell.

                One day, a small child played near the bank of a river, and listened to the flow of the water.  Danu saw the spark of recognition on the child’s face, and just then, she told the salmon to leap out of the water.  The child saw this and laughed, and Danu knew there was a connection.  “You must tell the others,” she beckoned with her rushing waters. 

                The Child told as many people as possible, “The river speaks.  The earth is alive.  The salmon leap in untold beauty.  Danu misses us!”

                Little by little, Danu’s message passed from person to person, and soon, the people gathered at the large Oak Tree.  They clasped hands, and closed their eyes, and listened.  A melody sung in their hearts grew louder, and they placed their hands upon their hearts.  They reached into their souls and felt a strand of light.  Each of them tugged on this strand, until it snapped free, and they held the light of song in their hands.  The light flowed and flowed, ever faster, until they could not hold it within themselves, and the light spilled into the earth, and seeped like sparkling water down, down into the Earth, to the roots of the great Oak Tree.  Deeper still, through the rocks, through the sediment, the light of the people sank into the heart of the earth, where Danu lay sleeping.  She awoke and felt the people once again, saw their faces alight with wonder, and tears of joy sprang forth from her eyes that her children could hear her again.  Her dark tears flowed, the tears of unformed Chaos, and filled all the waters of the earth.  The rivers, streams, and oceans rose, and the people felt the waters rise to their toes. 

When the people saw this, they were at first afraid.  “It's okay!" a child shouted. "It's Danu!"

And in their hearts they knew it to be true for the flow of the water didn't go around them, it went through them. And from their feet sprouts of energy grew, roots of light outstretched and beckoning back the love of their mother. The waters rose filling their feet and legs, filling a cauldron deep in their bellies they didn't know was there. The cool waters rose ever higher, soon filling a cauldron in their hearts where they felt the intensity of chaos, of possibility, of power potent and unformed. The waters rose still, spilling down the insides of their arms and hands, rising to fill a cauldron in their heads and they felt the knowledge and power of Danu's love.

 

They raised their faces to the sky as the waters poured out of the tops of their heads and each of them became a fountain of Danu. The people laughed and smiled and reached upward where they saw milky starlight pouring from the skies above. The celestial river of starry glistening Mother's milk poured down to each of them filling first the cauldron of their heads, then their hearts, and then their bellies.

 

And when this white hot firey starlight melded with the cool waters, a mist formed all around them.   The fog glimmered, pearlescent and cool, tingly to the skin. The people felt each other through the fog, knew each others’ presence, connected to all the worlds.

 

And Doubt and Fear, who had been living among the people, suddenly felt lost. They stumbled, reached out and sought the people, but could not find them, so they wandered away. 

 

And the people all stood together as one, and listened, and heard their mother speak.

 

"I am always here, children. You are part of me and I part of you, as we all are part of everything else. Always remember the magic of life."

 

And slowly the mists fell away and the people blinked and looked around. Everything was the same yet it was all so different. A world cast in a new light of beauty and understanding. And the people saw their access to greatness, to the power of the universe and knew that it was within them as well.

 

Still to this day, the people gather and listen to the trees, to the river currents, where the great mother, Danu, babbles through the brook and whispers on the wind. And when the people drink of her embrace, they know. They know. Children of the earth, may you always know.

Kaila: And now it is time to meet the Goddess.  Get comfortable, sit if you wish.  Breathe… Close your eyes… Come along.

 

You stand upon the banks of a beautiful, lazy flowing river. Your bare toes wriggle in the soft, fragrant soil upon the banks as you become attuned to the river’s flow. The sun warms you as it paints its dappled golden light onto your skin. It is midday. The only sound you hear is the slow, soft movement of water and the occasional buzzing of dragon fly wings at the water’s edge. Birds twitter and call to you from the tops of the trees far across the river and you fill your lungs with fragrant air – air that is alive with the scent of this rolling forested land…..deep rich soil….cool green water….plant life all around you. And now river beckons to you. “Come to me….flow with me,” she whispers on the current and you cannot resist. You move forward slowly, your senses taking in each new impression as you move forward slowly, dipping your toes into the cool, embracing water.

Happily, You begin to wade slowly into the river. It’s calm and gentle and you are not afraid. Feel the waters run against the flesh of your legs. You want to be in the water. You continue to move further into the water, unafraid. The soft wet soil feels so good under your feet…you continue to move. Now you are in the middle of the river and are standing up to your knees.

Let yourself ease back into the waters. She whispers her name to you as you lie back and begin to easily float. “Don, Danand, DANU.” Her voice is sweet and lulling – the voice of your Mother. Let yourself go. Let the Goddess hold you in her arms and carry you along. Feel the waters run over your body. Hear her voice whispering in your ears. Relax and trust her to carry you safely. Feel the buoyancy of your own body; spin with the river’s water; play with the Goddess. Dive down beneath the water if you wish. You can see clearly under the water and it is golden there with sunlight reflecting like liquid gold.

Continue to float down the river, enjoying the company of the Goddess. You look ahead and see an incredibly beautiful waterfall; as you follow the water up up up, naturally looking for the origination point of the waterfall, you see that curiously, this water does not fall from an Earthly place, but from the heavens. You are drawn to it and so you swim to the opposite bank from where you began and when you are ready you slowly emerge from the water, feeling the soft mist from the waterfall on your warm flesh.

As you step onto the bank of the river you see something moving in the grass. Unafraid, you see a beautiful golden serpent curled in the grass. You dare not move, so you continue to watch feeling the warm sun on your still-moist skin. The snake sees you, blinks slowly and moves away, back into the lush forest. Your eyes move back to where it lay in the sunlight and you see something there. What is it? You are drawn to it and so you make your way to that circular indentation in the sweet green grass and you see it. It is your gift from the Goddess. You pick it up and grasp it firmly. You are filled with joy as you stand there in the grass still feeling the mist on your skin.

Now you sense the sun moving slowly down toward the horizon. Now it is time for you to return to your starting place, and so you make your way back to the cooling water.

This time you have purpose. You enter easily, feeling the waters hold you buoyant. You being to swim upstream now, easily moving your body in the cool green golden liquid. Before long, you arrive at that wooded bank where your journey began. Still holding tightly to your gift, you place your feet into the rich wet soil and walk out of the river, back to the bank, back to the dappled sunlight and the buzzing of dragonfly wings.

Continue to hold your gift…..

And when you are ready, you may open your eyes and be back here in this room, still holding the gift from Mother Danu close to your heart.
